Support Wikipedia

Your donation protects the human right to free and open knowledge for everyone.

Donate now

Wikipedia is a free and open encyclopedia, hosted by the Wikimedia Foundation. The heart and soul of Wikipedia is our global community of over 200,000+ volunteer contributors, billions of readers, and donors like yourself – all united to share unlimited access to reliable information. Your donations keep our knowledge projects like Wikipedia freely available to everyone. Please help us keep Wikipedia growing.

Where your donations go

Transparency is core to the Wikimedia Foundation’s mission. Learn about how donations were spent last year to keep websites like Wikipedia thriving.

How we use your donations

Support from around the globe

Donors from around the world support over 300 Wikipedias, represent nearly 30 countries and more than 20 languages. Every country is different and we provide a variety of methods to make donations.

Ways you can give

Leave a legacy

Create a legacy that empowers free and equal access to the world’s knowledge. Make a future donation through your will or other estate plans.

Ensure the future of free knowledge

Our benefactors

We are grateful to the generous benefactors who have made significant donations. Large gifts ensure that Wikipedia and other Wikimedia projects have support for years to come.
