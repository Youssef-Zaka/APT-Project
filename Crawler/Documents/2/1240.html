Support and Grow Your Physics Community

Donate Now

When you support APS, you become a part of our vision to have a positive impact on the lives of people — researchers, educators, students and the general public — benefitting from APS programs. Since membership dues cover only the cost of member services, APS programs rely in great part on donations from generous individuals like you.

Your choice to support us is a personal one, and we aim to offer many opportunities to give in a way that is meaningful to you.

Featured Giving Option

Lars Onsager Prize

Your support will help to ensure the memory and unique spirit of Lars Onsager and his passion for analytical results live on by encouraging research in theoretical statistical physics, including in quantum fluids.

Onsager invented the theory of quantized vortices in superfluid helium and received the Nobel Prize in Chemistry in 1968 for his discovery of reciprocal relations in thermodynamics.

Donate to the Onsager Prize.
