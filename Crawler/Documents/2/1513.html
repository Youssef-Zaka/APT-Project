Education & Language

Springer Education & Language includes a comprehensive range of authoritative books, journals, and reference works covering key areas including higher education, learning and instruction, (applied) linguistics, mathematics education, professional & vocational education and science education. We invite you to explore our growing portfolio of education & language titles and find out more about the benefits of publishing with Springer.
