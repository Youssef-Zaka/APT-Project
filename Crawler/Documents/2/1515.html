How to read and search

Wiktionary is written to be used by readers like you, which is why we made it easy for you to search for definitions of English words.

There is a search box in the upper-right corner of every page with the word "Search" in it. Just type what you are looking for in there and then press "Enter ↵".

Getting and using a user account

A user account is not needed to read Wiktionary, but if you intend to change entries and be part of the community, creating an account is best to keep track of your changes. If you wish to create a new account, see Special:CreateAccount.

Helping the community out

Wiktionary is maintained by a community of editors, people who ensures that the definitions you see here are accurate and helpful. If you are keen to help out, do check out the following pages:
