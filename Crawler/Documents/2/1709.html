Materials

On these pages you will find Springer’s journals, books and eBooks in all areas of Materials Science, serving researchers, professionals, lecturers and students. We publish many prestigious journals in Materials Science, such as Journal of Materials Science, including a number of fully open access journals, such as Nanoscale Research Letters. Our book and eBook portfolio comprises major reference works, monographs, textbooks, conference proceedings and book series from the world’s most distinguished authors. Well-known publications include The Blue Laser Diode by Shuji Nakamura, recipient of the 2014 Nobel Prize, as well as Ceramic Materials and Transmission Electron Microscopy. Society partners include The Minerals, Metals & Materials Society (TMS), ASM International, and the National Institute for Materials Science (NIMS).
