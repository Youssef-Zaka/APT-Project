Order copies of U.S. Patent and Trademark Office documents

Use the Certified Copy Center to custom order Patent and Trademark documents from the U.S. Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO). Visit our frequently asked questions (FAQs) for additional information. Please vote, comment, and share ideas about the Certified Copy Center and its features, and tell us what you would like to see in the future.

Available products

Published patent documents

  • Patent documents
  • Patent assignment documents
  • Published patent application content documents

View the full list of published patent documents and fees

Trademark documents

  • Trademark documents
  • Trademark assignment documents
  • Trademark application content documents

View the full list of trademark documents and fees

Unpublished patent documents

  • Unpublished patent assignment documents
  • Unpublished application content documents

View the full list of unpublished patent documents and fees

Note: Copies of patent related documents that have not been issued or published are not available to the general public or open for public inspection; and no information or copies may be obtained without the written consent of the applicant, attorney of record, or the assignee. Documented entitlement or a power to inspect must accompany your request. During the order process you will have the opportunity to upload documentation that will be used for entitlement verification.

Account access or create an account

A USPTO.gov account is required to use the Certified Copy Center

Don't have a USPTO.gov account? Create an account now.

Existing online document ordering system users

You must have a USPTO.gov account to order certified copies of patent and trademark documents. To view your previous order history, link your USPTO.gov account to your online document ordering system account. Only online orders (not fax or email orders) are viewable online.

Already have a USPTO.gov account?

If you currently have a USPTO.gov account, sign in to the Certified Copy Center and during sign in, you can link your USPTO.gov account to your online document ordering system account.

Need to create a USPTO.gov account?

If you do not have a USPTO.gov account, create an account. Once you have an account, sign in to the Certified Copy Center and link your USPTO.gov account to your online ordering system account.
