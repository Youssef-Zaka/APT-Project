Compliance Management

In May 2015 Springer Science and Business Media merged with Macmillan Science and Education creating a major new force in scientific, scholarly, professional and educational publishing Springer Nature.

Springer Nature reaffirms Springer and Macmillan Science and Education’s commitments to respect the laws and regulations that impact our business and to act in a sustainable and socially responsible way. Over the coming months and years, our new company will continue to grow, adapt and evolve to meet the needs of the communities we serve but whatever we do will always be built on the same firm foundations that have led us so successfully to this point.

For information relating to Springer Nature’s Code of Conduct, our Supplier Code of Conduct and our approach to responsible business and compliance management please visit the Springer Nature website.

Springer’s professional Speak-Up system BKMS continues to be an important tool to encourage the early disclosure of risks or misconduct. It has been adopted by the whole Springer Nature group and is available to all employees and business partners to inform us of non-compliance, complaints about business standards or recommendations.
