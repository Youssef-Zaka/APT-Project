    United States Patent and Trademark Office

    PREFACE TO THE JUNE 2019 REVISION

    The June 2019 revision is a minor update of the June 2018 edition. Primarily, it incorporates relevant case law issued between March 2, 2018 and March 1, 2019.

    The title of the manual is abbreviated as "TBMP." A citation to a section of the manual may be written as "TBMP § _____ (2019)."

    As with previous editions, this edition is available online at the TTAB home page of the USPTO web site in a searchable, printable format as well as in pdf. A link to archived editions of the TBMP is also available at the TTAB's home page.

    Cheryl Butler

    Senior Counsel and TBMP Editor
