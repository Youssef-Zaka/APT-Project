Package Orders

Libraries have the option to purchase a subscription by title or subscribe to the entire MIT Press journals program or subject-specific packages. Complete package information and pricing can be found on Journals Packages or by selecting the Librarians option from the Resources link at the top of any of the Journals pages on our site.

Ordering Notes

Prices are subject to change without notice.

All orders must be accompanied by payment. We accept credit card (MasterCard, VISA, or American Express), check (must be drawn against U.S. bank in U.S. funds), or money order.

Subscriptions purchased at the individual, student, and retired rates are strictly for personal, non-commercial use only. The reselling of these subscriptions is strictly prohibited.

Where student subscription rates are available, a verification of matriculant status is required.

Subscription agencies must mail or fax their purchase orders to our Customer Service team to receive a discount.

Kinokuniya Company Ltd., www.kinokuniya.com, is our exclusive agent in Japan. Orders shipping to a Japanese institution or which are paid for by a Japanese institution must be sent to Kinokuniya.
