    Media Inquiries

    MIT Press is happy to provide information about our books and authors, as well as review copies of recently published titles, to representatives of the media.

    Requesting a Review Copy

    All requests should include:

    • Author and title of work
    • Your name
    • Name, address, and email of your publication

    Contact our Cambridge or London office as appropriate:

    In North America, South America, Australia, New Zealand, and Asia:

    Publicity Department
