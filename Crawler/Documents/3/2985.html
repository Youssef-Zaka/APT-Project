For the best Thanksgiving holiday ≫

Thanksgiving is upon us, and you know what that means: food, food and more food! Before you get stuffed like a turkey, though, you have to figure out what you are going to serve for your Thanksgiving dinner.

 

Would you rather make an easy, no-fuss turkey for Thanksgiving, or give your turkey a little pizazz by creating this fabulous rosemary-lemon turkey? Would you prefer a sweet potato casserole with pecan crumble or Greek yogurt mashed potatoes? (Why not both?!)

 

The sides don’t have to stop there. Be unique and make stuffing muffins instead of the typical all-American stuffing dish, and don’t forget the cranberry relish and delicious, creamy green bean casserole. We will provide the recipe, the rest is up to you!

 

At the end of the Thanksgiving meal, don’t forget your sweet spot and go for a rich, delectable dessert. Best of all: You don’t have to choose just one! Try the traditional deep dish apple pie, this amazing Gelt chocolate pecan pie, or be a little different and serve a pumpkin cheesecake roll. Make them all and be sure everyone will leave your home after the Thanksgiving meal feeling as stuffed as the turkey (in a good way).
