POPULAR WAYS TO GIVE
The Trust can help you support the causes you love.
Give and learn with others

The Trust hosts a number of giving circles, educational events, and collaboratives. Join our Giving Collective, a Funder Collaborative, or attend events.

Name The Trust in your will

It’s the perfect way to ensure your name lives on as a champion of the causes dear to you after you’re gone.

Learn more about leaving a bequest
Neighbors Helping Neighbors

With your help, our Community Needs Fund is helping address our city’s most pressing needs.

Support our Community Needs Fund
Choose an issue you care about

From supporting education to protecting the environment, you can create a fund in The Trust to make a meaningful impact on the issues that matter most to you.

Learn more about Field-of-Interest Funds
Give from your IRA

There are tax advantages for donors aged 70-1/2 or older who make contributions from their IRA directly to a public charity, such as The New York Community Trust.

Learn more about giving from your IRA
Create a donor-advised fund

You make gifts, and get an immediate tax benefit. Then you recommend grants to charities of your choice: We vet the nonprofits, write the checks, and handle the paperwork.

Learn more about Donor-Advised Funds
