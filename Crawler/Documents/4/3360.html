Digital Asset Management Software makes a difference.

LUNA makes it easy to organize, manage, and preserve digital assets.

 

Since 1993 Luna Imaging has been a trusted partner for museums, libraries, archives, individuals and all sized organizations looking to digitize and display cultural heritage materials.

Digital Asset Management Software: LUNA is a web-hosted software solution developed to display, manage and preserve all types of digital archive collections, image collections, special collections and library collections. The LUNA software is ideal for image files, video files, audio files, PDFs, books and manuscripts.

The LUNA Toolset Features:
Two viewer options for delivery
Data faceting for discovery
Collection categories for organization
+ Peer to peer sharing through IIIF
