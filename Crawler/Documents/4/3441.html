Abstract

This specification describes the definition of the Facets used in the Faceted Application of Subject Terminologies (FAST). As a subject authority system, most of FAST is adequately described using the Simple Knowledge Organization System (SKOS) descriptions.

Status of This Document

The FAST specification is produced as part of the FAST project, to provide authoritative documentation of the contents, status and purpose of its RDF vocabulary.
