  • Waleed Ammar, Matthew E. Peters, Russell Power
  • Published in *SEMEVAL 1 August 2017
  • Computer Science
This paper describes our submission for the ScienceIE shared task (SemEval- 2017 Task 10) on entity and relation extraction from scientific papers. Our model is based on the end-to-end relation extraction model of Miwa and Bansal (2016) with several enhancements such as semi-supervised learning via neural language models, character-level encoding, gazetteers extracted from existing knowledge bases, and model ensembles.
