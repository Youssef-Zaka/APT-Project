Donate

Donation with tax certificate via The King Baudouin Foundation

The Friends of Wikimedia Belgium Fund is recognised by the King Baudouin Foundation. Your donations will be used to finance Wikimedia Belgium's Fund-approved projects.

In principle, donations of 40 € per year to the Foundation give you a tax reduction of 45% on the amount actually paid (art. 145/33 CIR).

Belgium

  • Belgium has three regions: Brussels, Flanders, Wallonia.
  • There are three communities: German, French, and Flemish Community.
  • Belgium has three official languages: Dutch, French and German.
  • Wikimedia Belgium is ideally placed to interact with its sister organizations in France, Germany, Luxembourg, the Netherlands...
  • The capital of Belgium, Brussels, hosts European institutions like the European Parliament.
  • That is why we use English a lot.
