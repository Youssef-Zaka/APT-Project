Energy / BP selects UC Berkeley to lead $500 million energy research consortium
Global energy firm BP announced Thursday that it has selected UC Berkeley, in partnership with Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory and the University of Illinois, to lead an unprecedented $500 million research effort to develop new sources of energy and reduce the impact of energy consumption on the environment. The funding will create the Energy Biosciences Institute, which initially will focus its research on biotechnology to produce biofuels — that is, turning plants and plant materials into transportation fuels. History / Lessons from the bogs
Karin Sanders' life has been intertwined since infancy with "bog bodies," well-preserved remains from the Iron Age that surfaced in peat bogs in her native Denmark.
