      Recognition of Wikimedia Korea

      From the Wikimedia Foundation Governance Wiki
      ←Resolutions Resolution: Recognition of Wikimedia Korea Errors?→
      This resolution was approved on November 11, 2018.


      Whereas, the Affiliations Committee has assessed the application for recognition as a Wikimedia Chapter submitted by the Wikimedians of Korea User Group, and has advised the Board of Trustees that the applicant group meets the criteria for recognition as a Wikimedia Chapter, and that recognition of the applicant group as a Wikimedia Chapter under the name Wikimedia Korea would further the charitable aims of the Wikimedia Foundation and be in the best interests of the Wikimedia movement;

      Whereas, the Board has independently assessed that recommendation and agrees with it; and

      Whereas, the Board wishes to recognize and welcome the Wikimedians of Korea User Group as a Wikimedia Chapter dedicated to supporting and promoting the Wikimedia movement;

      Now, therefore, it is:

      Resolved, that the Board recognizes the Wikimedians of Korea User Group as a Wikimedia Chapter under the name Wikimedia Korea, provided that the Wikimedians of Korea User Group accepts and complies with a Wikimedia Chapter Agreement. The Board instructs the Executive Director to offer a Wikimedia Chapter Agreement to the Wikimedians of Korea User Group. This agreement will govern the chapter's recognition and provide the chapter with specific permission to use the Wikimedia trademarks.

      Approve
      Maria Sefidari (Chair), Christophe Henner (Vice Chair), Dariusz Jemielniak, James Heilman, Nataliia Tymkiv, Raju Narisetti, Esra'a Al Shafei, Jimmy Wales, and Tanya Capuano
