Geoffrey Plourde is a student from the U.S. State of California who has been a registered Wikipedian since May of 2006. He is currently working towards his undergraduate degree in business administration, while preparing to apply to business and law school within the next few years.

On Wikipedia, he has been involved in several initiatives over the past few years, including the Mediation Cabal, Association of Members Advocates, Esperanza, Motto of the Day, and Military History Wikiproject. Real world commitments consume most of his time, making him sporadically active here. Email is the best method to use to contact him, as it is the means of communication he will check most frequently.
