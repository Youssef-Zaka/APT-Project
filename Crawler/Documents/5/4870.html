About[edit]

I mostly work on articles about biochemistry, biophysics, and related topics. I am also interested in STEM education and in issues surrounding diversity in science. For the Cambrian fossil, see Opabinia regalis.

History[edit]

  • Wikipedia:Requests for adminship/Opabinia regalis - original RFA, 2006.
  • Wikipedia:Requests for adminship/Opabinia regalis 2 - second RFA, 2015. I think we decided this was the first successful re-RfA after desysopping for lengthy inactivity.
  • Wikipedia:Arbitration Committee Elections December 2015/Candidates/Opabinia regalis - arbcom candidacy, 2015. Elected to a two-year term, 2016-17.
  • Wikipedia:Arbitration Committee Elections December 2017/Candidates/Opabinia regalis - arbcom candidacy, 2017. Re-elected to a two-year term, 2018-19.
  • Wikipedia:Arbitration Committee Elections December 2021/Candidates/Opabinia regalis - arbcom candidacy, 2021. Elected to a two-year term, 2022-23.
