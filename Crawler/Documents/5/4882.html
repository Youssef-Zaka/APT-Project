Hello, I'm a digital librarian, recently relocated to beautiful Edmonton, Alberta. I contribute mostly in English but understand and participate en Francais.

I work closely with the Art + Feminism and 1Lib1Ref campaigns each year and helped develop some of the programming for Wikipedia:GLAM/McGill. My interests include digital culture, open access/copyleft, personal and community digital archives and photography.

I'm always interested in connecting with community groups that want to get involved in Wikipedia and don't know where to start. I'm available to help plan events, point to resources and lead training sessions.
