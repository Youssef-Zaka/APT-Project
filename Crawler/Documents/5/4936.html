My name is Anna, I'm a Hungarian administrator (2014–) and bureaucrat (2016–) on the Hungarian Wikipedia. I started editing it in 2010, since then I became a member of the Hungarian chapter of the Wikimedia Foundation and I was elected as board member in 2016.

If you would like to know more about me, please feel free to visit my primary user page.
