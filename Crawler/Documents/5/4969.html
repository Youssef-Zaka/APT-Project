Wikimedia IRC has been migrated from freenode to Libera Chat. For more information, please see meta:IRC/Migrating to Libera Chat. References to freenode on this page and others may be out of date.

There are three IRC channels for users of Simple English Wikipedia hosted on Libera.chat:

Channel Description
#wikipedia-simple connect General chat about the Simple Wikipedia. Usually on-topic.
#wikipedia-simple-admins connect For discussion between administrators (admins only)
#cvn-simplewikis connect Anti-vandalism channel for the Simple Wikis (Wikipedia and Wiktionary). Not for questions or reporting vandalism.

There is a channel that broadcasts the events in New changes. If you do not want to use a client and use just the web, you may do so by going to Libera.chat Web Login.
