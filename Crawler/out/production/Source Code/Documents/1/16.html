Greek[edit]

Noun[edit]

μηχανική • (michanikí) f (uncountable)

  1. (physics, mathematics) mechanics

Adjective[edit]

μηχανική • (michanikí)

  1. Nominative, accusative and vocative singular feminine form of μηχανικός (michanikós).
