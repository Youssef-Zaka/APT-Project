What is civil engineering

Civil engineering is everything you see that's been built around us. It's about roads and railways, schools, offices, hospitals, water and power supply and much more.

Designing, creating and connecting the world

Civil engineers help make our villages, towns and cities work for the people that live there. Have a look at all our civil engineering projects and see for yourself the work that civil engineers do.
