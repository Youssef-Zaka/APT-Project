      Welcome

      Thanks for being here!

      This wiki does not exactly follow the same rules as the other Wikimedia projects, since it is not open to all for editing, and in case of disagreement, the mechanism for making decisions will be handled by the Wikimedia Foundation staff or Board of Trustees.

      It is managed by Wikimedia Foundation staff and contractors, primarily within the Communications and Legal departments.

      Scope

      Main article: Wikimedia:Scope

      This wiki makes publicly available multiple governance and policy related materials from the Wikimedia Foundation, such as:

      • Board of Trustees documentation
        • Minutes
        • Resolutions
      • Legal documentation
      • Organization policies
      • Security documentation

      It also stores the archives of the no longer actively used or maintained Foundation Wiki content.

      Accounts on this wiki

      Main article: Wikimedia:Accounts policy

      In October 2021, all registered accounts on other Wikimedia project wikis got edit access to the talk and user pages of this wiki and became able to support translation of this wiki's content into languages other than English.

      Editing on regular content pages is restricted to approved editor accounts.

      Administrators on this wiki

      Administrators previously included a variety of Foundation staff and non-Foundation volunteers. From May 2013, only Foundation board and staff members may be administrators on this site (though temporary adminship for non-Foundation personnel may be possible in certain cases).

      Stewards and global sysops have administrator rights on this wiki.
