Promotional Materials

Project MUSE is transitioning from a fully remote work environment to a hybrid arrangement, with limited time in our offices. We are able to fulfill materials requests, but there may be delays in shipping due to staff availability and postal delays that we cannot control. We suggest making requests for physical materials well in advance of the date you need them.

We also recommend you consider using the wealth of downloadable/digital resources on the Project MUSE web site:

https://about.muse.jhu.edu/resources/user-guides/

Thank you for your patience and understanding.


Subscribing libraries or libraries conducting a trial of Project MUSE and MUSE sales agents and publishers may use this form to request promotional materials to help promote MUSE.
