Register

Find a program near you and register online today! If you’d prefer to register by mail, download our registration form and then complete and mail your registration form to our headquarters.

Refer Your Friends

Join our Refer a Friend program and help friends save $30 when they register their kids for camp. You’ll earn an Amazon.com Gift Card each time a new family registers with your unique link. Camp is more fun with friends, so click below and start sharing!

Get Ready for Your In-Person Camp
Together with friends, your innovator is about to have the best experience of the summer! Check out the resources below as you prepare for all the fun of your child’s in-person camp.

Prepare for Your In-Person Program

Send your innovator on a scavenger hunt for recyclable items they’ll use for prototyping at camp. You’ll also need to prepare a lunch, drink and snack each day (unless notified otherwise), and be sure to provide any medication information on the first day.
