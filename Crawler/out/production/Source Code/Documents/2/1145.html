FAQs

How do I redeem a Very discount code?

  • Click on any offer with a ‘view voucher’ button for a code to appear on your screen. So that you can complete your order, you will be directed to very.co.uk in a separate tab.
  • Fill your basket with whatever items you would like to purchase and head to checkout.
  • Under your order total, you will locate a box labelled ‘promo code’. Enter your code there and your discount will automatically be applied!
  • If your Very discount code does not work, please check to see if the code has expired or if the items you would like to purchase don’t fit the terms and conditions.

What is the best Very promo code?

Our favourite promo code ever was 20% off orders of £100 or more. What made this discount so great was it applied to all fashion items. Another sought after Very offer is £30 off orders over £60, which applies to new and existing customers. Check out our promotions to see how you can save today!

Is there a Very student discount?

Unfortunately, there is no student discount at Very. We work with UNiDAYS and Student Beans. Should an offer become available, you will find it here!

Does Very have an NHS discount?

There is no NHS offer or Blue Light discount at Very. That doesn’t mean you can’t save, though. Enjoy 20% off or more by viewing our current voucher codes.

How can I get free delivery at Very?

There are a couple ways that you can enjoy free delivery at Very. First, you can opt for their Click & Collect service, which is free on all orders of £30 or more. There are thousands of Collect+ locations and post offices that you can pick up your order free of charge, usually the next day.

Alternatively, there are occasionally Very free delivery codes. These codes are usually for specific items or brands. We recommend the Click & Collect service for most orders; especially if you are interested in next day delivery.

Tips to shop smarter at Very

Save with a Very discount code

Very’s online catalogue is massive, with a huge selection of fashion, appliances, electricals, beauty and more. Whether you want to buy a new rug for your living room or get a pair of boots for a fun night out, you can find it at Very. Usually, such large retailers only offer codes for specific categories when they have such a large selection. For instance, electrical or clothing.

Luckily, our exclusive Very discount codes give you sitewide savings. The cashback discount code gives you £25 off when you spend £125 or more. What are you waiting for? Treat yourself to a new coat, that Oculus Quest 2 or AirPods without feeling guilty.

Enjoy every sale in one place

Our team of voucher experts work hard to bring you every Very sale to ensure you always get the best price. We promote flash sales like an extra 20% off sales items, broader sales such as up to 50% off home furniture and specific promotions like Up to £50 off iPhones and Apple Watches. Through research, we know Very is a popular retailer for items like the Dyson Airwrap, air fryers, Shark Hoovers and other various items. If these products go on sale, you will be the first to know about it.

Very Pay – Buy now, pay later

Do you need to make a bigger purchase and would like to split the payments? Very Pay offers a Buy Now, Pay Later service that ranges in pay back plans from 6 months to 12 months. This is related to Credit accounts and does come with interest. Very does not work with Klarna and other popular buy now pay later services in the UK.

Helpful info

Customer service contact information

The Very customer service team is there to help you through anything from warranty requests to questions about returns. You can contact them via phone at 0800 11 00 00. For those who don’t enjoy phone calls, the Very online chat is a quick and easy way to receive help. To access the Very assistant, you will need to login to your account. This chat is available 24/7.

The Very return policy

You will be happy to know that all returns are free at Very and can be completed within 28 days of receiving your order. To make a return, you will need to login to your account and visit the ‘My Orders’ section. You can select the items there that you would like to return and choose which option best suits you. After this, you will be able to print the Very return label and all that is left is dropping off the package at a post near you.
