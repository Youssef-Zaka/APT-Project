COVID-19

 

In response to the global coronavirus pandemic, the semiconductor industry is focused on ensuring the health and safety of its workers, contributing to the effort to combat the virus, and sustaining the continuity of the critical systems and technologies that underpin modern society.

Semiconductors enable vital parts of our economy, infrastructure, and national security, and they are at the heart of many breakthrough technologies being used to overcome this global health crisis.

Below are some key SIA resources related to COVID-19, as well as examples of the actions taken by SIA member companies in response to the crisis.

For more information about how to help fight the spread of COVID-19, please visit: www.fema.gov/coronavirus/how-to-help. To learn more about government resources available to support businesses and employees, please visit: https://home.treasury.gov/cares
