Public Health

On these pages you will find Springer’s journals, books, and eBooks in all areas of Public Health, serving researchers, healthcare professionals, lecturers, and students. We publish many of the most prestigious journals in Public Health, including a number of fully open access journals. Our books and eBooks portfolio comprises major reference works and textbooks from the world’s most distinguished authors such as Boris Sobolev, Richard A. Kaslow, Seth Kalichman, and Zili Sloboda. Well-known publications include: International Journal of Public Health, AIDS and Behavior, Maternal and Child Health Journal, Implementation Science, Advances in Prevention Science, and SpringerBriefs in Public Health. Society partners include: International Society for Urban Health, The Swiss School of Public Health+, Society for Prevention Research, and National Society of Genetic Counselors.
