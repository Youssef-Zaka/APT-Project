Motivación

As of now, we have no way to link a Vikidia article with a Wikipedia article, a Commons category, or any Wikidata data. These properties will allow us to do that, using SPARQL queries. I'll only propose identifiers for Vikidia editions that contains at least 500 articles, so there are three Vikidias that I won't propose (Russian [195 art], Greek [45 art], and Sicilian [39 art]).--Tinker Bell ★ ♥ 05:58, 17 December 2019 (UTC)[reply]
