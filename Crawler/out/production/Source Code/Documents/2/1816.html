New Prairie Press Follow

New Prairie Press at Kansas State University offers a home for scholarly publications edited or authored by scholars committed to the principles of open access publishing. While the Press focuses on journals, monographs, and conference proceedings in the humanities, social sciences, and the arts, publications from all disciplines are welcome.
