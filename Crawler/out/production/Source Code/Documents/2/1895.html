WHAT IS CIVIL ENGINEERING?
Civil engineers have more of an impact on daily life than you might think.
Find out which people and projects have made a difference to society.

With more than 95,000 members worldwide, ICE supports civil engineers throughout their careers.

We award professional qualifications that are the industry standard, lead the debates around infrastructure and the built environment and provide an unmatched level of training, knowledge and thinking.

Rishi Sunak at One Great George Street
