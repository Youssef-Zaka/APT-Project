NOTE: This page has become disorganized. Please organize categories and pages under the categorization schema. Pages and subcategories may be sorted under larger headings. For example: Aerospace engineering is a subcategory of Mechanical engineering, which is in turn a subcategory of Engineering. Also, please note that the example category names follow the Wikiversity Naming Conventions and consist of only the first word and "proper names" capitalized (such as "15th century maps of Asia" or "How to install a Timing-belt"). Similarly, all Topics and Learning Resources within a particular category should be changed to reflect only the category that they are in. For example Topic:Civil engineering should be categorized under Category:Civil engineering and the Learning Resource Strength of materials should be categorized under Category:Solid mechanics.
