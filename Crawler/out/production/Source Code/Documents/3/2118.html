About Thomas Telford

Thomas Telford is the knowledge business of the Institution of Civil Engineers (ICE), creating specialist products and services for the civil engineering and construction markets. These include technical books and journals in both print and digital format and the NEC® suite of contracts. We provide recruitment services including the ICE Recruit job board and technical training for ICE members and those in the wider construction sector. See and click below for more information on our products and services.



ICE Publishing

Authoritative books and papers from world renowned authors, available in both print and digital formats.

ICE Recruit

ICE Recruit is the UK's number one specialist civil engineering job board and the official job board of the Institution of Civil Engineers (ICE).

NEC®

NEC is a family of contracts that facilitates the implementation of sound project management principles and practices as well as defining legal relationships.

One Great George Street

One Great George Street is a conference, banqueting, meeting, exhibition and wedding venue in central London.

ICE Training

ICE Training provides specialist training for civil engineers, project managers and construction professionals.
