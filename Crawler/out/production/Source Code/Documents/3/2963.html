About Curlie

Curlie is a directory of the web that is 100% maintained by volunteer editors all around the world.

It exists to help people find web content that they value the most. It covers a full range of topics such as Movies, Games, Food, Travel, News, and Sports.

Since we're all volunteers, we need funds to keep the site running.  Anything that you can contribute would be greatly appreciated.

Meanwhile, please visit our site at curlie.org and explore all of the topics that interest you.

Thanks!

- The Curlie Crew
