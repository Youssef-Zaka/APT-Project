Power through your day.

The  durable HP Chromebook 14 G7 has the Chrome OS, Intel® processors1, future-ready USB-C™ and HDMI, a long-running battery, and the optional HP Privacy Camera.2

Damage-resistant reliability

This resilient device undergoes MIL-STD 810H testing3 and features long battery life, a minor-spill-resistant keyboard, and reinforced ports and internal structures for durability over time.

Work uninterrupted and charge fast

Spend more time getting your work done instead of looking for an outlet with a long battery life and HP Fast Charge, which recharges the battery to 90% in 90 minutes.5

Designed for easy collaboration

Collaborate with a narrow bezel screen, wide-angle HD webcam, and dual mics. Add your monitor or accessories with HDMI and USB-C™. Navigate content in low light with the optional backlit keyboard.2

Easily master your day, in the office or on-the-go.

Multi-task with the quiet, fanless, ultra-light, and thin design with up to 128 GB eMMc.4 Collaborate with a lie-flat chassis, webcam, and audio.

Cut through network clutter with enhanced wireless.

  • Choose your screen experience

    Select a 14” diagonal, non-touch, anti-glare HD or FHD screen,2 HD touchscreen2 or anti-glare FHD touchscreen.2 Each has a narrow bezel so you see more of your work.

  • Optimized for the cloud

    Use multiple tabs and apps7 simultaneously and store files locally on a Chromebook with up to 8 GB memory2 and 128 GB eMMc storage.4

  • World-class support

    Rely on support you can trust with HP’s world-class limited warranty. Extend your protection to cover accidents, travel, next-business day needs, and more with optional HP Care Pack services.8

  • Faster, more resilient wireless connectivity

    Get enhanced connectivity even on crowded wireless networks at a long range with Wi-Fi 6 WLAN11 module, supporting MU-MIMO12 and HP Extended Range Wireless LAN.13

  • USB-C™ simplicity

    Charge and transfer data to and from USB-C® devices and accessories. USB-C® ports provide high compatibility with a variety of optional Chromebook accessories.2

Enterprise-grade security and manageability made simple

Save IT time and money with the simplicity of enhanced Chrome Enterprise Upgrade15 manageability and security.
