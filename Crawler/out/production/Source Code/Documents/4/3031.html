The enterprise-ready Chromebook. Powerful, stylish, intuitive.

Easily work in the cloud with performance and privacy features. The HP Pro c645 Chromebook Enterprise has full Chrome OS3 manageability and security for IT. 

Powered for Enterprise

Confidently complete projects with a powerful AMD Ryzen™ processor1 and integrated graphics. Boot up and access apps faster2 with a high-speed solid-state drive.

Connect with intuitive, dynamic features

Easily collaborate and work in multiple tabs with the impressive graphic performance of an AMD processor1.

Optimized for the cloud

Use multiple tabs and apps3 simultaneously and store files locally on a Chromebook built for the enterprise with up to 16 GB memory4 and 128 GB PCIe® NVMe™ M.2 SSD storage4,5.

A simple way to manage

Get seamless device integration with the Chrome Enterprise Upgrade3. Get 200+ policies and Active Directory, VDI, and enterprise mobility management support7. 

Elegantly designed for style, portability, and durability

Thin and light, with an aluminium exterior and sanitizable backlit keyboard, this Chromebook easily goes where your day takes you. 

Hear, share, and capture with crystal clarity

Make the most of your collaboration with Audio by B&O, an integrated wide-view angle HD camera, and HDMI port to easily add a projector without a dongle.

USB-C® simplicity

Charge your Chromebook and USB-C® accessories with two USB-C® ports. Get an optional HP USB-C® dock10 to connect to a wired network and USB-A accessories.

Work uninterrupted and charge fast

Spend more time getting your work done instead of looking for an outlet with long battery life and HP Fast Charge—recharge the battery 90% in 90 minutes7.

Features

Preserve your privacy
 

Get peace of mind with a built-in HP Privacy Camera that blocks the lens of your camera.

Secure your access
 

Unlock the screen the optional, integrated fingerprint sensor2.

Hardware-enforced verified boot with multi-level encryption

Get verified boot protection, two versions of Chrome OS, and encryption for your Chromebook, files, and user. Remotely deactivate if it's lost or stolen3.

Seamless updates

Stay protected with updated OS and security patches that are pushed11,12 every six weeks without causing end-user downtime.

Faster, more resilient wireless connectivity

Faster, more resilient wireless connectivity enabled by 2x2 Wi-Fi 69.

Top-firing speakers with Audio by B&O

HP top-firing speakers, custom-tuned in collaboration with experts at B&O, provide sound clarity for more effective conference calls.
