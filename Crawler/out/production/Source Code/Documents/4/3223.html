Our UK office

Our bright, modern offices are based over two floors and are fully air-conditioned. We are centrally located, just a five-minute walk from the train station and a 10-minute walk into the centre of Bristol, if you fancy popping out at lunch to grab a sandwich.

Directions to our office

By air: Bristol International Airport is approximately 30 minutes’ drive from our offices and has regular scheduled flights to all parts of the UK and Europe.

By rail: Bristol Temple Meads railway station is approximately five minutes’ walk from our offices and is a mainline station with regular services to all parts of the UK.

By road from the M32: At junction 3, continue along the dual carriageway, which is a continuation of the motorway, and at the road junction turn left onto the dual carriageway (signposted Temple Meads railway station). Following A37, A4, Temple Meads, go under the underpass, straight on at the two sets of traffic lights, and approach the roundabout in the right lane. At the roundabout take the third main exit to double back onto Temple Way. Our offices are on the left, next to the Hilton Garden Inn.
