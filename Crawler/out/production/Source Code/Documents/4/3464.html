Cambridge English Qualifications


Schools

For us, learning English is more than just exams and grades. It’s about having the confidence to communicate and access a lifetime of enriching experiences and opportunities. Our qualifications are designed specifically for school-aged students, giving them regular milestones to keep them motivated.

Working with Cambridge Assessment English means access to a comprehensive range of exams and support. We provide exam preparation and free resources to help teachers prepare students, and – as the world’s leading provider of teaching qualifications and courses – we help teachers to continually develop their professional skills.

Offer Cambridge English Qualifications

Cambridge English Qualifications

Assessment helps schools and students to reach the highest standards. As language learning can be a long process, it’s important to have regular milestones to aim for – our exams provide focus and pace to keep your students motivated, every step of the way.

Find out more about our exams

Support for teachers and learners

We provide a comprehensive range of exam preparation, support materials and resources for teachers who are preparing students to take our exams. From handbooks and sample papers to monthly webinars and seminars, we provide all the classroom support you need to help your students reach their potential.

Find out more about our resources

Teacher training and development

Better teaching leads to better outcomes for learners and we can help support teachers every step of the way. As the world’s leading provider of English teaching qualifications and courses, we offer a comprehensive range of training and support to help teachers continually develop their professional skills.

Find out more about teacher development

Working together

It’s easy to start working with us – there are no long registration forms. Simply contact your nearest authorised exam centre; they will give you all the information you need to get started, including learning resources, training, and marketing materials.

Find out how to become a preparation centre

Let’s look in more detail at the learning journey

We offer a seamless journey from Pre A1 Starters to C2 Proficiency. Each exam is carefully designed to consolidate the skills developed at the previous level, and lay the foundations for future success.

  1. Pre A1

    Pre A1 Starters

    This is the first of three exams for young learners. All three exams help younger children take their first steps towards learning English.

    More information
  2. A1

    A1 Movers

    This is the second exam created specifically for younger children. All our exams for young learners are fun, colourful and activity based, motivating children to learn.

    More information
  3. A2

    A2 Flyers

    This is the third exam created specifically for younger children. These exams build young learners’ confidence and lay the foundations for future success in English.

    More information
  4. A2

    A2 Key for Schools

    This qualification helps learners to fully develop their everyday written and spoken English at Level A2. The exam builds on the skills students have achieved at A2 Flyers.

    More information
  5. B1

    B1 Preliminary for Schools

    This is an intermediate-level qualification for students who have mastered the basics of English and now have practical language skills for everyday use.

    More information
  6. B2

    B2 First for Schools

    This qualification is a great way for learners to prepare for higher level exams, and shows they have the language skills needed to communicate confidently in an English-speaking environment.

    More information
  7. C1

    C1 Advanced

    This is proof of high-level achievement in English and the ideal qualification for students preparing for university or professional life.

    More information
  8. C2

    C2 Proficiency

    This is our highest level qualification and it shows the world your learners have mastered English to an exceptional level.

    More information
