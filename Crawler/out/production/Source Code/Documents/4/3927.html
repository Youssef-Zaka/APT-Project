OUP's Response to COVID-19 Learn more

Dear Customer,

As a global organization, we, like many others, recognize the significant threat posed by the coronavirus.

During this time, we have made some of our learning resources freely accessible. Learn more about these useful resources on our COVID-19 page.

Our distribution centers are open and orders can be placed online. Do be advised that shipments may be delayed due to extra safety precautions implemented at our centers and delays with local shipping carriers.

Please contact our Customer Service Team if you have any questions.

International Customers

Domestic Shipping

Our standard method of shipping for US orders is through USPS. Standard shipping charges for US orders is $5.50 for the first book and $1.50 for each additional book.

Back to top

Heavy Book Charge

Certain items, due to weight, may incur additional shipping charges. Details regarding additional fees are provided on the product page and will be automatically reflected in the shopping cart at checkout.

Back to top

International Customers

Our standard method of shipping for orders outside the US is international mail. Standard shipping charges for orders outside of the US is $10.00 for the first book and $5.00 for each additional book.

In some cases where other shipping charges may apply, these charges are noted on the product page and will be reflected in the shopping cart at checkout.

Other methods of shipping including expedited services are available via telephone orders. If you order by phone, your customer service representative will provide you with the details including the additional shipping charges. Shipping via UPS Mail takes 2-8 weeks (depending on your location) once the order has shipped from our Warehouse.
