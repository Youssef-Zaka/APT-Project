Acer Chromebook Enterprise Spin 514

Thin & Light Performance

Buy Now >

Coming with the Chrome Enterprise Upgrade already unlocked, the convertible Acer Chromebook Enterprise Spin 514 lets you accomplish more at work with its AMD Ryzen™ processor, military-grade durability, and a 14” touchscreen display.

The Power to Perform

Multitasking on this Chromebook is a breeze with the latest AMD Ryzen™ 5000 Series processor based on AMD’s Zen 3 architecture and integrated AMD Radeon™ graphics.

Chrome Enterprise Upgrade

Chrome Enterprise simplifies the way you run your business. Cut costs, simplify operations, reduce security risks, and greatly lower required IT resources by being able to deploy, update and manage all your devices at any scale.

Zero-touch Enrollment

Featuring zero-touch enrollment, IT departments will be able to drop ship Chrome OS devices that will be automatically enrolled into enterprise administration as soon as the user connects to the internet.

All-day Battery Life1

10 hours of battery life goes a long way. With just a single charge, get in a full day’s work with time to spare without having to take a bulky power adapter with you.

Superior Screen

With a 78% screen-to-body ratio and ultra-narrow 6.1mm left and right bezels, you can immerse yourself in a stunning 14” FHD IPS touchscreen display with 100% sRGB2 protected by Corning® Gorilla® Glass.

Built to Last

Worry less about your device so you can focus on your work. Durable and long-lasting, this laptop boasts independently verified military-grade (MIL-STD-810H3) durability, standing up to all the rigors of daily workplace usage.

Designed for Video Calls

This Chromebook features an FHD webcam with a camera shutter for privacy protection. When combined with the two upward-facing speakers, its dual-microphone array, and HDMI 2.0 port, users get an even better video call experience.

Complete Connectivity

This Chromebook is packed with functional features including Wi-Fi 6, an optional backlit keyboard, and dual USB 3.2 Gen2 Type-C ports to deal with everyday usage.

Tougher, Smoother Touch

Tapping, swiping, scrolling and zooming are smoother than ever. The Corning® Gorilla® Glass touchpad provides you with the optimal input control you expect from a durable, high-quality Chromebook.
