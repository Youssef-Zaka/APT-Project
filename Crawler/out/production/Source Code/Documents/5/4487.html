Thanks for your interest in “Navigating hybrid work: A Google Workspace handbook.” Learn about the tools, spaces, and best practices that can connect your in-office and remote teams, and which will help you plan and succeed on your own hybrid work journey.

Read how Google learned from its own experiences and working with customers to:

  • Keep people productive and engaged, even when they’re away from the office
  • Prevent the sort of meeting fatigue that happened during the pandemic
  • Make the hybrid work experience equitable for all employees
  • Ensure the in-office experience promotes collaboration

The handbook highlights existing and soon-to-be-released features in Google Workspace to help you transition to a hybrid work strategy today.

Download the handbook now.
