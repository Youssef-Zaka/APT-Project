This category concerns the topic: terms related to engineering.

It contains no dictionary entries, only other categories. The subcategories are of two sorts:

  • Subcategories named like “aa:Engineering” (with a prefixed language code) are categories of terms in specific languages. You may be interested especially in Category:en:Engineering, for English terms.
  • Subcategories of this one named without the prefixed language code are further categories just like this one, but devoted to finer topics.
