This category concerns the topic: terms related to industries.

It contains no dictionary entries, only other categories. The subcategories are of two sorts:

  • Subcategories named like “aa:Industries” (with a prefixed language code) are categories of terms in specific languages. You may be interested especially in Category:en:Industries, for English terms.
  • Subcategories of this one named without the prefixed language code are further categories just like this one, but devoted to finer topics.
