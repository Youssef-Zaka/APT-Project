Saramin increased organic Search traffic 2x

In addition to seeing a traffic increase, Saramin saw the quality of their traffic improve. They saw a 93% increase in the number of new sign ups and a 9% increase on the conversion.

Case study

MX Player boosts organic traffic 300x

After adding Video structured data and following best practices, MX Player saw over 300X growth in traffic from Google and a 100% increase of video page views per user session on their platform from organic Search traffic.

After the launch of JobPosting structured data, Google organic traffic to ZipRecruiter job pages converted at a rate three times higher than organic traffic from other search engines.
