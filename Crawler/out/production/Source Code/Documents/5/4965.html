Tips for chromebook adoption

Is your organization switching to Chromebooks? Here are five time-tested tips for driving Chromebook adoption. Learn more.

Free from the mundane. Free to innovate.

Secure by design, easy to manage, and low in cost, Chrome devices set IT free from the many day-to-day pains of just managing endpoints — all while providing your employees fast and seamless access to information and cloud-based tools.

Free to keep your data protected

  • Protect against current threats. Mitigate the impact of malicious attacks prevalent on legacy systems.
  • Keep harmful apps out. Managed Google Play puts IT in control, helping ensure harmful apps stay out of users’ hands.
  • Devices stay up to date. Regular system updates happen seamlessly in the background so users are protected.
Learn more  arrow_forward

Free from tedious tasks

  • Fast deployment. Fast and easy deployment enables organizations to quickly scale with less IT burden.
  • Systems that behave. Reduce calls to the help desk with a reliable OS that’s easy to use and always up-to-date — thanks to regular background updates.
  • Take the guesswork out of IT. Our well-researched default settings take the hassle out of management.

Free to get the job done

  • Start fast, stay fast. Devices boot in under six seconds1, while updates occur in the background.

    • Boot up speed varies depending on device.

  • Work flexibly. Secure, cloud-based backup systems and eight+ hours of battery life2 let employees work worry-free, from any location.

    • Battery life may vary based on device and upon usage and other conditions.

  • Pick up where you left off. Save and sync apps, preferences, and extensions to stay productive on any Chrome device from anywhere.
