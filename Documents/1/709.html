The Wikimedia Foundation Annual Plan is the plan of record for the Wikimedia Foundation's work each year. These are scheduled for the Wikimedia Foundation's fiscal year, which starts at July 1 and ends at June 30 of the following year. The creation of the plan may sometimes be referred to as the Annual Planning Process or APP.
