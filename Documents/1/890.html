About me

Hello. I am a first-generation American, born in Indiana, grew up in Los Angeles, and now I live in Northern California.

At a glance...

  • 2007, English Wikipedia editor (administrator, 2009)
  • 2014, founder, WikiProject Women Writers
  • 2015, co-founder, with Roger Bamkin, Women in Red
  • 2015, Volunteer of the Year, Wikimedia District of Columbia (WMDC)
  • 2016, Wikimedian of the Year, with Emily Temple-Wood
  • 2016, shortlisted, ITU/UN Women GEM-TECH award
  • 2016-21, WMDC Board member; Vice-president, 2017-2021
  • 2016-21, member Wikimedia Foundation Affiliations Committee; Secretary, 2018; Chair 2020-21
  • 2017-present, Wikimedia Visiting Scholar at Northeastern University, University Libraries' Digital Scholarship Group, Women Writers Project
  • 2017, contractor, Wikimedia Foundation, Gender Diversity Mapping project
  • 2018, knighted by Serbia
  • 2018, member, Wikimedia Foundation, Community Health Working Group
  • October 23, 2021: Wikimedia Foundation Trustee
