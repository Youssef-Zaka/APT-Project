Project[edit]

16 October, 2018 - 30 October, 2018 This is a collaboration between Wikimedia Armenia and Wikimedians of the Levant. The idea to have this cooperation is a part of Cultural Exchange Program Wikimedians of Levant User Group experienced in September 2018. Wikimedians from both communities will translate articles from respective languages.

The highest article translator from each language will receive a postcard from Armenia and Jordan as a token of appreciation.

Time[edit]

  • 16 October, 2018 - 30 October, 2018 (for Armenian wikieditors as they have other project in November)
  • 16 October, 2018 - 16 November, 2018 (for Arabic wikieditors as they are students involved in other collaboration and have exam period in the first 2 weeks of this cooperation)
