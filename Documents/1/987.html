f-number (P6790)

From Wikidata
Jump to navigation Jump to search
ratio of the system's focal length to the diameter of the entrance pupil. Sample: 4 (generally written "ƒ/4") for a lens with 200 mm focal length using an entrance pupil diameter of 50 mm.
