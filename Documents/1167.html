The Future of Work Summit

Reimagining the FoW with automation, collaboration, and communication technology

The post-pandemic workforce is demanding a new level of flexibility, which has set into play two new driving forces. First, remote work is pushing enterprises to invest in hyper-automation of many of their processes, as well as embrace of increased digital team collaboration. Simultaneously, the advance of virtual technologies like AR and VR are helping accelerate the emergence of a metaverse, which itself is being accelerated by hybrid work and the need for people to be more virtually immersed. What do these forces mean for technical decision-makers?

At VB’s Future of Work Summit, we’ll discuss with top executives how organizations are reimagining and re-architecting their technology stack and strategies to allow for this new virtual world of work. This transformation enables powerful, transformative AI/ML, metaverse technologies such as AR/VR, and AI-assisted intelligent chat and virtual assistants for more collaboration. But it also requires a new kind of workplace culture, emphasizing trust and independence. The Summit will extract specific takeaways, including strategies, tactics, technologies that the boldest and most successful companies are leveraging to pave the way for this new future of work.

Topics To Be Covered

Enterprise Automation

The changing relationship between the workforce and technology.

Remote Working

Understanding the importance of tech-driven collaboration and how to build a thriving culture.

New Technology

Case studies and benefits in productivity and growth through tech such as RPA, NLP, VR/AR, and more.
