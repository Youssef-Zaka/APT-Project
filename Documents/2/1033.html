DescriptionPetra Al-Kaznah by Night.jpg
English: Al-Khazneh, "The Treasury", is one of the most elaborate temples in the ancient Arab Nabatean Kingdom city of Petra. The structure is believed to have been the mausoleum of the Nabatean King Aretas IV in the 1st century AD. It is one of the most popular tourist attractions in both Jordan and the region. It became to be known as "Al-Khazneh", or The Treasury, in the early 19th century by the area's Bedouins as they had believed it contained treasures.
