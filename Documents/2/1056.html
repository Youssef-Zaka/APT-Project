The Movement Strategy and Governance team of the Wikimedia Foundation will host a global conversations event on the concept of Hubs on March 12, 2022. It is the second event focusing specifically on Hubs, the first one was the co-creation workshop on November 27, 2021.

Event details[edit]

Findings summary of the Hubs Dialogue, for presentation at the 12th March Global Conversations.

This Global Conversations event will bring Wikimedians together to discuss the findings from the Hubs Dialogue: a series of conversations facilitated by the Movement Strategy and Governance team designed to answer the question: What are the generally shared and context-specific needs among the Wikimedians exploring the hub concept?

The aim of the event is to share the findings of the Dialogue with the Movement, ensure that they reflect Wikimedians’ lived experiences, and from this shared understanding, start discussing how the findings can be integrated into the Movement Charter drafting process.

What do we expect from participants? We will share the findings of the conversations held with groups that are working on establishing a Hub in their context and we would like participants to listen in, understand, and reflect on these findings.

Date + Time: 13:00 to 16:00 UTC, March 12, 2022

Program: see below

Simultaneous interpretation: Arabic, French, Spanish, Russian, and Portuguese

Registration: Closed on March 10!

Questions? Comments? Feedback? Send the organizers an email to strategy2030wikimedia.org

Program[edit]

Second draft of the program (updated on March 9)

Time elapsed Content
0:00 - 0:10 Intro + Welcome
0:10 - 0:30 Presentation of Hubs Dialogue findings
0:30 - 0:50 Poll + discussion on shared needs
  • For each shared need: Does this identified shared need align with your observations of or lived/expected experience with hubs?
  • Discussion
    • What are your initial reactions to seeing this result?
    • What does this mean for the development of hubs moving forward?
0:50 - 1:00 Break
1:00 - 1:55 Discussion on how hubs will address shared needs
  • Discuss one shared need at a time, is this essential or an additional attribute?
1:55 - 2:05 Break
2:05 - 2:55 Discussion on how hubs will address shared needs (continuation)
  • Does this shared need correspond to an essential or preferred attribute of hubs?
2:55 - 3:05 Summary of next steps and wrap up (END)
3:05 ~ 4:00 Social time in virtual corridors (break out), if you like!
