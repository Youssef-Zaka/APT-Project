The Wikimedia Foundation Trust and Safety Policy team hosted a Panel Q&A with community members¬†on 18 February 2022 at 15:00 UTC to discuss the Universal Code of Conduct (UCoC) Enforcement Guidelines and the need for the community‚Äôs involvement in the upcoming Ratification vote. A special focus of the event was why it is important for small and medium size Wikimedia communities to participate in the ratification vote.

The recording is available to watch on Commons and YouTube.

The panelists are: Ruby D-Brown, Isaac Kanguya/Icem4k, Uzoma Ozurumba, and Nahid Sultan. This event is part of our efforts to engage, educate and inform the Wikimedia community about¬†the UCoC, the Enforcement Guidelines as well as the upcoming Ratification vote for the UCoC.

We kindly ask you to invite other volunteers in your community to get them informed and empowered to vote!
