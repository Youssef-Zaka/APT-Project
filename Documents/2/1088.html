ParameterDescriptionTypeStatus
Languagelang

Indicates the standard tag (based on an ISO 639-1/2/3 code, sometimes followed by a BCP 47 subtag) of the language used by the translated template.

Example
en
Auto value
{{#invoke:Caller title|lang|base={{subst:FULLPAGENAME}}}}
Linesuggested
Page languageuselang

Indicates the standard tag (based on an ISO 639-1/2/3 code, sometimes followed by a BCP 47 subtag) of the language of the wikis to link to, by default the template language.

Example
en
Auto value
{{CURRENTCONTENTLANGUAGE}}
