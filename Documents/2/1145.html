English: The Prehistory period — the human period before recorded history.
  • The Prehistory period relates to three consecutive time periods in prehistory (dates varying by region or continent), named for their respective predominant tool-making technologies:
  1. Stone Age: (Paleolithic -> Mesolithic -> Neolithic -> transitional Copper Age)
  2. Bronze Age
  3. Iron Age —> (then recorded history — classical antiquity)
  • For more, see also wikipedia: article (english text): Timeline of prehistory; and category (english text) en:Category:Prehistory.
