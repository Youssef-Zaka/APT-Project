2022

  • April 20: The course Embracing Ambiguity is now available and is part of the Possibilities curriculum.
  • April 8: Students at Memorial University of Newfoundland are documenting public folklore for the town of Harbour Grace.
  • April 7: Students at the University of North Carolina Chapel Hill are contributing to Open Teaching of Psychological Science.
  • April 5: The course Solving Problems is now available and is part of the Possibilities curriculum.
  • 31 March: Students at the University of Maryland, College Park are extending Helping Give Away Psychological Science and Evidence-based assessment.
  • 24 March: The essay Is Wikipedia a legitimate research source? has been published and is ready for viewers.
  • 11 March: The philosophy course Finding Common Ground is now complete and ready for learners!
  • 23 February: Students in ENG 105 at the University of North Carolina Chapel Hill are contributing to Federal Writers' Project – Life Histories.
  • 19 February: Students at the University of North Carolina Chapel Hill are learning about psychological science and evidence-based assessment.
