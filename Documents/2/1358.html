Script Summary: Pops up a warning if you click the Edit button while not logged in with a registered account on Wikipedia or any other Wikimedia Foundation site, and will not proceed. Useful for users who are worried about accidentally exposing their IP addresses. To install on Firefox, install the Greasemonkey extension from https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/greasemonkey/ then return here and click Install. To install on Chrome, download the js file by clicking Install, visit chrome://extensions/ in your browser, and then drag it onto your browser.
