My user name is Kippelboy and I am one of 125,393 active users who are building the 6,494,701 pages of this project. I am a Wikimedian based Catalonia, and member of Amical Wikimedia. I am mainly active on Wikidata and Catalan Wikimedia projects, where I started editing back in 2007. As an educational background, I do have a degree in Business administration and two postdegrees, one in Museum Management and another one in Cultural policies.

Until the lions have their own historians, the history of the hunt will always glorify the hunter.

— Chinua Achebe, The Art of Fiction No. 139
