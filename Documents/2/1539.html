Description

Conference Management Toolkit (CMT) is one of the most versatile and scalable conference management systems. Conferences with many thousands of papers run smoothly on CMT's cloud-based platform every day. The app allows authors to keep track of submitted papers on the move, and receive notification from chairs about their paper's status. Reviewers can browse and bid on conference papers, view the PDF of assigned papers inside the app, view others' reviews, participate in discussion and receive notification.

Please send feedback to support@msr-cmt.org.
