Welcome to WikiProject MoMA on the English Wikipedia!

We are a group dedicated to improving Wikipedia's coverage of topics related to the Museum of Modern Art (MoMA) in New York City.

  • Members describes membership from Wikipedians, MoMA staff, and education partners,
  • Challenges describes article challenges and prizes, lists of requested new and improved articles, and the monthly collaboration.
  • Events describes live events, outreach and educational collaborations, in the past, present and future.
  • Tasks describes positive outcomes and requested articles for creation.
  • Discussion is a place for comment and requests from Wikipedia oldies and newbies alike.
