WikiAfrica Culture and Knowledge aims to encourage new and existing Wikipedians to create and expand the current information on African Culture on Wikipedia. The project aims at inviting cultural experts, writers, critics, publishers, cultural institutions, new Wikipedia users, and the Wikipedia network to increase the number of the African culture entries present in the free Wikipedia Encyclopedia. The Cultural project asks all those who know about events, organizations, festivals and cultural history to contribute their knowledge to this project.
