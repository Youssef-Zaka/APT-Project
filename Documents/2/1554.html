WikiAfrica Incubator

Welcome to the WikiAfrica Incubator!

The WikiAfrica Incubator is designed to support and assist new authors as they create their first articles in Wikipedia. It follows the basic rules and principles of Wikipedia. More experienced users can help develop these articles until they are ready to be moved into the main namespace.


Propose an article topic here.

Get Involved:
Create a new account and become a registered Wikipedian. Make 10 edits to existing Wikipedia articles. Click here for a list of articles on Africa needing expansion. Review the Wikipedia guidelines and the steps of using the Incubator. Create your own article and share your knowledge of Africa.
