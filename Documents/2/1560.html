Smithsonian Institution Archives Wikimedia Partnership
From March 2012 until June 2012, SarahStierch, Wikipedian-in-Residence, worked closely with archives and digital media staff at the Smithsonian Institution Archives (SIA) to expand archival content and partnership in and with Wikimedia. This page served as a hub for all Wikimedia community events, content organization, and community collaboration. Currently the Archives is collaborating with local Wikipedians and staff to hold edit-a-thons.

Current updates[edit]

Participate as a volunteer and you could earn the official SIA barnstar!

Welcome to the project page for the Smithsonian Institution Archives partnership! This will be a great opportunity for Wikipedians around the world to help improve content on Wikipedia and related websites utilizing SIA's vast resources. SIA covers everyone and everything related to the Smithsonian Institution, and the people who helped make it what it is.

You will find value in participating in this project if you have interests in:

  • Museum history
  • Science
