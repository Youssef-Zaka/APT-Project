About Us
▼

Data Engineering - Data infrastructure, datasets, pipelines, and related services.

We develop infrastructure so all our users can access data in a self-service fashion that is consistent with the values of the movement. We provide the Wikimedia analytics data platform, making Wikimedia related data available for querying and analysis to both WMF and the different Wiki communities and stakeholders.

The Wikimedia Foundation's Data Engineering team is part of the Technology department.
