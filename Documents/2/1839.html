Proceedings Point

Conferences are a vital source for research exchange. We publish over 1,200 conference proceedings per year, either in dedicated series, as standalones, or as part of mixed book series, with experience dating back to the 1970s.

Our prominent partners are for instance NATO, IFIP, IACR, and many other societies and organizations.

We welcome proceedings for the academic, corporate, industrial, and government market.
