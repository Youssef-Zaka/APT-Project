    Make Epic Happen
    Whether you are an Entrepreneur, Creative leader, Student or someone who gets things going at the workplace, the Mi Notebook 14 is here for you to Make Epic Happen.

    The Mi Notebook 14 is very compact, thin, and weighs just 1.5kg. A beautifully designed 35.56cm (14) FHD Anti-glare display with thin bezels gives you an immersive viewing experience that you will love. Equipped with the 10th Generation Intel® Core™ i5 Processor, it’s a productivity powerhouse that lets you multitask like a Pro.

    Whether it’s binge-watching, coding or designing, you will experience the Mi Notebook 14 delivering on the promise of speed and performance. With 7-8 times faster transfer speeds than normal HDDs, faster boot-ups and game-loads, it comes with a 512 GB SSD. Designed for an effortless typing experience, it comes with scissor-switch keys with a travel distance of 1.3mm, making typing more precise than ever. With all of this and also a 46Wh Battery with 10 Hour Backup, we’ve got you covered for the whole day.

    The Mi Notebook comes with a convenient USB Plug and Play 720p HD Webcam, which slides seamlessly over the thin bezel above the screen, helping you take those work-from-home and learn-from-home calls with ease. It’s also super easy to detach and carry in your bag, or leave at home, when not required. Interesting thing to try: Move the camera around to show your work or study place or introduce your family to your colleagues or friends
