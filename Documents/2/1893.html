Our Bookshop

The Cambridge University Press Bookshop in the heart of the city centre is a unique shop on the oldest bookshop site in the country.
We are proud to showcase a huge range of the Press's publishing as well as selling a wide selection of gifts and souvenirs.

We offer price promotions and discounts throughout the year - see our Discounts and Special Offers page for more information.

Follow us on Twitter, Facebook and Instagram for all the news and more.
