Summary[edit]

DescriptionSEACComputer 024.jpg NBS and the U. S. Patent Office had, at the time of this photograph, begun investigating a program called HAYSTAQ for automatic searching of chemical literature. It was expected to reduce the time and effort required in searching through a file of patents. Ethel Marden is pictured at the control console of SEAC, where some of the experimental work on HAYSTAQ was performed.
