Summary[edit]

DescriptionAbstract Wikipedia and wiki of functions.webm
English: An animation that very briefly explains the Abstract Wikipedia project and what "functions" are. It shows how topics that are missing in one language, might created by functions (including sentence constructors) written by contributors. Content includes File:The_Earth_seen_from_Apollo_17.jpg (public domain) and icons from OpenMoji (CC BY-SA 4.0).
