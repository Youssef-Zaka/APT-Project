Shipping Time

 

Please allow 4-6 weeks from mailing date for U.S. delivery, and 6-8 weeks for international delivery.

 

Claims

 

Requests for replacement copies due to non-receipt must arrive within three months (U.S.) or six months (international) of publication date or date of order (whichever is later). In all other cases the single-copy price will be charged for replacing an issue.

Individual subscribers: Log in to your account to claim a missing issue.
Institutional subscribers and agents: Email subscriptions@press.uchicago.edu to claim a missing issue.
