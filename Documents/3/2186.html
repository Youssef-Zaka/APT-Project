Call for proposals: Legacy Landscapes Fund

Monday, 31 January 2022 at 17:00
access_time 1 min read

Legacy Landscapes Fund (LLF) has announced its first open call for proposals.

The open call is from 31 January 2022 - 30 April 2022.
For detailed information, please make sure to carefully read all documents accessible below.


Process of LLF Call for Proposals 2022

What LLF funds and How to Apply

LLF will hold open Q&A sessions about the application process for the 2022 Call for Proposals in February and March. Sessions are voluntary, but LLF encourages eligible grantees to participate. As of the end of February, there will be a revolving Q&A document available.
