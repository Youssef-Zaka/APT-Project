Creating new possibilities in higher education

College and university campuses are vibrant, complex ecosystems of learning and discovery across disciplines and campuses. See how Google for Education helps you manage the complexity and keep everyone connected and productive.

  • Talk to a specialist

Powering higher education research and infrastructure

Google solutions create the powerful computing infrastructure that keeps today’s higher ed communities humming. Researchers can speed up analysis from days to minutes, working seamlessly across departments and data sets. Students and faculty can collaborate easily and securely across disciplines and campuses. And campus staff can work more efficiently and effectively.

Learn how a research team at the University of South Carolina uses cloud technology to study climate change. Read more.

See how a Brown University professor used VR to bring American history to life. Read more.

New ways to collaborate and innovate

From smarter email to seamless video conferencing to real time feedback on student work, Google for Education productivity solutions fit seamlessly alongside higher education tools and systems, helping students and faculty be more efficient and productive. With virtual reality technology, faculty and students can co-create immersive experiences for deeper exploration and learning.

Secure technology with tools for easy IT management

IT administrators can set up and manage custom policies across school and student devices in just a few clicks. Google’s robust admin and technical controls make it easy to monitor and manage applications system wide, freeing up IT staff to spend less time on manual technical support and more time on institutional priorities.

Learn how Lafayette College saved time and costs by switching to Google Workspace for Education Plus. Read more.
