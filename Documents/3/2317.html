How our Web Design Service works

See below for our simple site building process

Fill out our Getting Started Form

Youll provide details on you and your project, and our team will review and schedule a call.

Call to Connect

On a call with our team, we’ll discuss your website in detail – understanding your concept and goals. Based on the call, we’ll send a proposal detailing all aspects of the project. If approved, we’ll move forward with the next phase, ‘Design and Build’.

Design and Build

Our expert team takes your concept and design direction, turning your vision into your new website, powered by WordPress.com. Well work with you to make sure the site matches your vision. While going through the design and build phase, you will have opportunities to review and provide feedback along the way.

Launch your new site

Once you review and approve your new website, it’s ready to launch! We’ll continue to support any follow-up questions you have. Show the world your next big idea.

Frequently Asked
Questions

Fill out the Get Started form. We’ll then contact you for more information, and begin building your site.

What is Built By WordPress.com?

Built By WordPress.com is WordPress.coms custom web design service. You tell us about the site you envision, and well work closely with you to design and build your site. The end result will be fully customized for you.

What is the cost?

Our website design & development services start at $2,000 USD. The price will vary depending on the number of pages, design specifications and additional functionality (i.e. eCommerce, Membership, etc.) Our consultation team will help you refine requirements and provide you a quote tailored to your unique needs.

What is the average time to build a website?

Our average project timeline is 4-6 weeks, though sometimes it can be longer given project complexity. Have a shorter timeline? Reach out and we can help expedite. 

Who works on my website?

We have a WordPress.com team that will handle your project from start to finish. Youll have a dedicated account manager who will gather your feedback and facilitate the development process. The team also includes expert developers who will design and build your website to fit your specific needs and goals.

Where is my new website hosted?

It will be hosted on WordPress.com. 

Do I own my website after the work is completed?

Yes, the website is yours once it’s finished. You can add and edit copy, images, and more, all with the power of WordPress.
