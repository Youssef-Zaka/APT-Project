FAQs

What is P2?

P2 is a flexible platform for teams to share, discuss, and collaborate openly and without interruption. It helps closed communication become more transparent and rich vs. restricted documents or email. P2 is built with WordPress & Gutenberg.

How does Automattic use P2?

Automattic is an office-less, globally distributed company with over 1200 employees in over 80 countries. P2 is our lifeblood and we have been using it to collaborate on projects without constant distractions for a decade.
