Let your pictures tell the story.

They’re worth a thousand words, and with our Google Photos integration, they’re even easier to share.

Get Started

All of your pictures in the cloud.

Google Photos is an easy way to store and back up your photos. Get the app on your phone, take some photos, and access them later online. No manual uploading required.

Get Started
Share on your site
Link your Google Photos account to your WordPress site and get access to your whole photo library. Pull up your last 10 images, or search your entire catalog. It’s a quick and easy way to go from shooting to sharing.
Copy to your library
When you share multiple photos at once from your Google Photos library they’ll be copied over to your WordPress library, making it easier for you to use throughout the site on other pages and posts.
More than photos
Google Photos stores more than still images. You can also share videos, animations and collages created through the app.
Access every picture
Access isn’t limited to just your photos. Download images from your shared albums and libraries too. Perfect for teams working across multiple devices.

WordPress and Google. Better together.

With your Google Photos account linked to your WordPress site, you can seamlessly share the pictures you take on your phone, or any device connected to your Google Photos account.
