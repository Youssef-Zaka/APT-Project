Dimensions Analytics

Dimensions Analytics provides access to Dimensions’ complete, comprehensive, and connected data set and supports complex data analysis to meet the needs of academic institutions, government organizations, research funders, publishers, and R&D departments.

Request a demo

Dimensions Analytics provides access to Dimensions’ complete, comprehensive, and connected data set covering publications, grants, patents, clinical trials, datasets and policy documents. In addition, it supports more complex data analysis to meet the needs of academic institutions, government organizations, research funders, publishers, and R&D departments. We even provide a configuration that enables you to securely integrate your own confidential data.

With Dimensions Analytics, you enjoy:

  • Access to our complete dataset covering publications, grants, patents, clinical trials, datasets and policy documents.
  • Access to the full-text PDF versions of more than 30 million Open Access articles with just one click.
  • An extensive set of filters, including specialized category systems, Open Access, publishers, and funder groups
  • More advanced options for building structured searches and to define your own groups of entities
  • Enhanced visual data analysis and exploration capabilities
  • Increased export limits per download
  • An email alerts feature

Enhanced discovery tools

Dimensions Analytics aggregates data across all content types to deliver a full picture of past, current, and future research. Article-level research categories are consistently applied to all content types, as well as funder and research organizations. In comparison to the free version, Dimensions Analytics offers greater export limits for standard xlsx/csv exports and bibliometric mapping with VOSviewer and CiteSpace. You can also view Altmetric online attention for individual items, or aggregate the data for both publications and clinical trials.

Workflow support

Dimensions Analytics contains a reviewer identification tool, which includes committee building, committee assignment, and clustering for manuscripts and applications. We also offer advanced portfolio reporting functionalities for research funders and publishers, and coding support for several category systems.

Access and data integration

We support several authentication options: IP whitelisting, EZproxy and Single-Sign-On (Shibboleth etc.). This ensures your users can view Dimensions data at any time and from any location. We can also can set up a physically separate application for your organization and integrate private and confidential data for your analytical needs and reviewer identification or provide custom implementations and integration with your existing systems.

API access

Dimensions APIs not only offers access to the database’s underlying data, but the business logic layer too. This allows you to customize the standard interface to meet your specific needs.

Support and consulting

We are here to help, whenever you need us – our team offers a 24/7 support service. To help you make the most of Dimensions’ rich data, we also offer a range of information materials, host regular webinars, and invite you to submit your questions. In addition, our consulting team can work with you to ensure you are maximizing the potential of your Dimensions Analytics subscription.

Interested in learning what we can do for your organization? Get in touch with our team to request more information.
