JACC: Case Reports Accepting Applications For Structural Heart Disease/ACHD Associate Editor

JACC: Case Reports is inviting applications for an associate editor specializing in structural heart disease, with a focus in adult congenital heart disease. The ideal candidate will have expertise in these clinical areas, a strong track record of original scientific contributions, and experience in editorial and peer review. Apply by June 13. Learn more.

Research from HRS 2022

Six manuscripts coinciding with HRS 2022 presentations have published in JACC and JACC: Clinical Electrophysiology, including a state-of-the-art review, an innovations in basic/translational electrophysiology manuscript examining a first in human feasibility study, two original research manuscripts and two research letter. See all six simultaneous publications.

JACC Edge: Read the New Issue

The second issue of the JACC Edge newsletter, "Heart Failure, with a Touch of Guidelines," provides a look at heart failure content from across the family of journals, including takeaways from the new Heart Failure Guideline, research from the PROMPT-HF trial, the JACC: Basic to Translational Science Technologies and Heart Failure Therapeutics Shark Tank 2022 special issue, and more. Read the new issue online and subscribe to receive future issues of the newsletter at JACC.org/Alerts.

Incoming Editorial Board Members Named

Incoming JACC: Heart Failure Editor-in-Chief Dr. Biykem Bozkurt, will be joined on the editorial board by Deputy Editor Dr. Akshay S. Desai, and Associate Editors Drs. Tariq Ahmad, Anita Deswal, Paul A. Heidenreich, Nasrien E. Ibrahim, John McMurray, Sean P. Pinney, Muthiah Vaduganathan, and Mary Norine Walsh, MD, MACC. The first issue under the new Board will publish on June 27. Meet the Incoming Editorial Board.
