Community

 

 
 

Wikimedia / Wikipedia is a worldwide community,
that aims at providing access to knowledge
for every human being.

 
 
 
 


Play media
Promotional video about free access to Wikipedia, featuring a school-class from South Africa and their open letter to tele-cos (Wikipedia Zero).

 
 
 

 


  

"Imagine a world
in which every single person (...)
is given free access
to the sum of all human knowledge.
That's what we're doing."

Jimmy Wales, Wikiquote

  






  • Wikipedia is created by a community of volunteers ("Wikipedians"), who try to be friendly and civil towards each other.

 
 

Play media
A short video about people like you who edit Wikipedia. (1:26 min)
