Financial Reports

The Wikimedia Foundation is a donor-funded, US 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization established in the United States under the US IRS Code Section 501(c)(3). Our tax ID number is 20-0049703. Here, you can read our financial reports and our Form 990s.
