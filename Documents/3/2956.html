    DescriptionFemale Aircraft Engineers, Kwara State, Nigeria.jpg
    English: This picture was taken at the International Aviation College, Kwara State, Nigeria. It shows female aircraft engineers working on an aircraft. These women are in a male dominated profession but scaled through the tides and are successful in the field. There few female working in the engineering section of aviation in Africa.
