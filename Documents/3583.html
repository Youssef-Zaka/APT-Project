    Overview[edit]

    Wikibase is a MediaWiki extension (and a suite of associated software) that lets you store, manage and access structured data. It's also the software that powers Wikidata. Technically it is two separate extensions:

    • Wikibase Repository - the main extension
    • Wikibase Client - used on other wikis that integrate with the structured data repository wiki
