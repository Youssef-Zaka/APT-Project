About Provost Kotlikoff

It is my honor to serve as Cornell’s 16th provost, where I act as the university’s chief academic officer and chief operations officer. In consultation with President Pollack, the vice provosts, university deans and other senior leadership, my office oversees Cornell’s teaching, research and outreach, ensuring that we advance these cornerstones of the university mission.
