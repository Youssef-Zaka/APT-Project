Land Acknowledgement

Cornell University is located on the traditional homelands of the Gayogohn' (the Cayuga Nation). The Gayogohn' are members of the Haudenosaunee Confederacy, an alliance of six sovereign Nations with a historic and contemporary presence on this land. The Confederacy precedes the establishment of Cornell University, New York state, and the United States of America. We acknowledge the painful history of Gayogohn' dispossession, and honor the ongoing connection of Gayogohn' people, past and present, to these lands and waters.
This land acknowledgment has been reviewed and approved by the traditional Gayogohn' leadership.
