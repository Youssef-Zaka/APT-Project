About us

Press Office

Research and learning are the cornerstones of progress, which is why we open doors to discovery – enabling millions of researchers, educators, clinicians and other professionals to access, trust and make sense of the latest insights. Ensuring that the world makes as much progress in the next 150 years as it has in the last – improving and enriching lives and helping to protect our planet for future generations.
