My work

Here is a partial list of works that i coordinated or involved in :

Past and Ongoing Works

  • Project manager, Nigeria Language Oral History Documentation
  • I co-created Wikimedia User Group Nigeria
  • I participated in Wiki Indaba 2014
  • I participated in WikiAfrica/OpenAfrica15#OpenAfrica15, a conference for Wikipedians In Residence, organised by WikiAfrica in Johannesburg, South Afria
  • I coordinated Wiki Loves Africa in Nigeria
  • I conceptualized Wikimedia Usergroup Nigeria/Adopt a school series in Nigeria
  • I coordinate official mailing list of WUGN and Facebook of the group in Nigeria (WUGN facebook page)
  • I Coordinated Celebrating Wikipedia @15 in Nigeria
  • I coordinated the Wiki loves Nigeria Writing Contest, a fallout of Wikipedia @15
  • I coordinated the Wiki Loves Women in Nigeria
  • Wiki Photo Tour in Nigeria, coming up in March-July 2016 Wiki Photo Tour in Nigeria
  • I conceptualized media partnership between Wikimedia UG Nigeria and Blackface Media, a radio program that runs every Wednesday called Time-Out with Wikipedia.
  • I am the lead presenter on Wikimedia Radio and TV program
  • I coordinated Wiki Loves Earth 2017 in Nigeria
  • I coordinated Wiki Loves Monuments 2016 in Nigeria
  • I conceptualized the planning of Wikimedia Fan Club at Lagos State University in Nigeria
  • I conceptualized the establishment of Wikimedia Fan Club, University of Ibadan
  • I conceptualized the establishment of Wikimedia Hub, Nigerian Institute of Journalsim (NIJ)
  • I conceptualized and sealed agreement for the execution of Wikipedian in Residence at Goge
  • SHE CAN with ICT
  • Wikipedian in Residence at Goge Africa/Timeline
  • I organized with my team WikiMaster Conference in Lagos
  • I organize local version of Black History Month [here]
  • Project coordinator, [https://meta.wikimedia.org/wiki/Visibility_Project
