User:Isaac (WMF)/Link scripts

I have developed a series of user scripts as an exploration in data visualization, making research models/data more accessible, and showcasing the importance of article links. They all take the basic form of highlighting links on a Wikipedia article based on some criteria. The first script was built by User:TayIorRobinson and all others since then have been simple tweaks.

For examples of how to enable them, see: https://meta.wikimedia.org/wiki/User:Isaac_(WMF)/global.js

Scripts[edit]

  • Links + gender
  • Links + clicks
  • Links + quality
  • Links -> QIDs

See Also[edit]

  • Presentation that I gave on the original link gender script at the Wikimania 2021 Hackathon: video (Youtube)
  • Presentation that I gave on research and the role of links at WikiConference North America: video (Youtube) + slides
  • Not links, but another user script that predicts the quality of Wikipedia articles and recommends improvements.
