Emily Lescak
Senior Research Community Officer, Wikimedia Foundation

About me

I joined the Wikimedia Foundation in June 2021. I live in Texas, USA.

My work

My research background is in fisheries biology. My focus at the Wikimedia Foundation is on strengthening and expanding the community of researchers around the Wikimedia projects.

Disclaimer: Disclaimer: I work for the Wikimedia Foundation, and this is the account I intend to use for edits or statements I make in that role. However, the Foundation does not vet all my activity, so edits, statements, or other contributions made by this account may not reflect the views of the Foundation.
