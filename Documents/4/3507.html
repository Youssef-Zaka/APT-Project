Membership

Over 500 institutions have partnered with us to help authors maximize their research funding. Researchers affiliated with these member institutions may receive a discount on article processing charges (APCs).

Check if your institution is a member in the list below and learn how you could benefit from APC savings when publishing with us.

If your institution is not currently a member, visit our parent company’s OA funding & support services page to learn more about APC funding options and our support service for authors.

The membership is applicable to BMC and Springer fully open access journals only, and does not apply to any Nature Research, Palgrave, or Springer Nature hybrid open access (Open Choice) journals.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact: info@springeropen.com.
