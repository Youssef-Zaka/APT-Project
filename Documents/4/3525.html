Research Areas at Meta

At Meta, research permeates everything we do. We believe the most interesting research questions are derived from real world problems. Working on cutting edge research with a practical focus, we push product boundaries every day. At the same time, we publish papers, give talks, and collaborate broadly with the academic community.

We solve real-world problems that impact billions of people in areas such as:

AR/VR

Creating the future of personal and shared reality

Artificial Intelligence

Advancing the field of machine intelligence

Blockchain & Cryptoeconomics

Changing the way money moves

Computational Photography & Intelligent Cameras

Mobile visual processing to understand, augment, and enhance sharing and connections

Computer Vision

Understanding the visual world around us

Data Science

Gaining insights to deliver meaningful social interactions

Databases

We build the next generation of data systems that help connect the world.

Economics & Computation

Exploring the intersection of economics and computation

Human Computer Interaction & UX

Understanding and improving social experiences

Machine Learning

Applying machine learning science to Facebook products

Natural Language Processing & Speech

Breaking down language barriers

Networking & Connectivity

Data networks that support the social network

Security & Privacy

Keeping the Facebook community safe and secure

Systems & Infrastructure

Building the systems to bring the world closer together
