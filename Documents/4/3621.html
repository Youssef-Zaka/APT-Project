Description

Wondrium is a place where you can let your mind go. Wondrium provides an expansive collection of educational videos to help you learn about new topics. Learning new topics has never been easier.

Wondrium offers thousands of new online learning experiences, providing videos, documentaries, lessons, series, and more. With topics spanning across math, science, history, biology, crafts, art, music, personal development, chemistry, foreign language, English, French, Spanish, German, and other categories, you’ll have vast opportunities to learn what you love and love learning about it.

All our educational streaming videos are academically comprehensive, relentlessly entertaining, and led by engaging experts. If you’ve ever wondered about anything, Wondrium will be your new favorite place to go. With amazing content partners like Intelligence2, CLI Studios, Commune, Craftsy, Magellan, Kino Lorber and many more, you’re sure to find content that fits your interests.

Whether you’re a beginner in a subject or advanced in your knowledge and skills, whether you want to begin binge-learning deeply on one topic or sample a few select categories, there’s something here for you. Whether your interest is watching documentaries; discovering the latest breakthroughs in science, math, biology, or chemistry; or learning photography, piano, knitting, painting, French, Spanish, German, or anything in between, there’s something here for you.

If you don't already have an account, you can register and subscribe right in the app. With a subscription, you can access thousands of educational videos online and offline for one low price.

This app works best on iOS 12.0 and higher.

olamk , 07/13/2021

Great content and features, despite usability impairments

I love this app and service. I like that new content is being added and I particularly like the audio-only mode. I will be a subscriber for long time, but have three more (necessary) usability requests and two suggestions. (1) allow users to browse the app while audio programs are playing. Right now, the only way to browse for other content within the app is to stop the program. This does not follow the iOS HIG. Also, please change the player to the native iOS media players. (2) performance improvements, particularly background battery drain. I’m unsure why this app is such a battery hog, even when content is downloaded in the background. (3) for browsing, a better system is needed. I’d suggest filtering options (including one for “percent seen.” The current “Netflix” style of browsing is painful and slow. These are just ideas, but I think they would be awesome to add after the usability problems have been addressed! (4) note-taking during content and live closed captioning should be added and integrated for future reference, given the service’s educational focus, this would be a boon for studying or looking back on past materials. (5) ratings and reviews for content, simple thumbs up, thumbs down. Would help in finding quality content when there is overlap in available programming.
