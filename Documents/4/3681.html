Advanced Services

Whether you are still growing your data science practice, or you are deploying machine learning to for commercial solutions, our team of experts can help improve performance and recommend changes to application code and architecture to maximize performance and deliver better outcomes.

OSS Services

Are you using one of the open-source packages Anaconda helps to maintain, some of which are listed on our Open Source directory? If you have ideas on how those projects could be improved to better meet your needs, Anaconda has expert developers on staff who can help improve and extend them to be more useful for everyone, getting your job done quickly without having to build or maintain a fully custom solution.

Integration Services

Do you need help interfacing Anaconda software to other systems, e.g. to handle your local authentication, data access, or other integration needs? If your needs are not covered by one of the kickstart packages, we can develop a custom Services project to help you get the most out of our software.

Conda-build Services

Are you building workflows around conda, but periodically find that you need to access new packages not yet available for conda? We are happy to set up an ongoing set of hours for building new packages by request, ensuring that the packages include all the metadata needed for them to work well in your environments.

Other "ad-hoc" Services

Anaconda focuses on building general-purpose commercial and open-source tools usable across many different domains, industries, and research areas. If you have a more specialized need for some truly custom work, feel free to get in touch anyway! If it is not in an area that we work on directly, Anaconda can introduce you to one of our trusted Python software development partners.

Custom Engagements

Sometimes organizations need a unique solution. Our open-source team will create conda packages just for you, and our engineers will carry out custom development projects to meet your unique needs.
