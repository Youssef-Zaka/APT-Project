Mun May Tee-Galloway
User Experience/Visual Designer, Wikimedia Foundation

About me

Apart from the Muni F-line light rail, what brings me to work every weekday is the thought of working with a genuine organization and people who care about their products and their impacts on society. A place where I become a better citizen by building and improving purposeful products that mould society into becoming more conscious and knowledgeable.. which in turn benefit bigger things than just us humans. I care about giving a damn and I do so with design.

My design career started with a fortune-teller who saw my future in the advertising design world in 2006. What happened from then was what felt like a series lucky strikes. It kicked off with a gig at J Walter Thompson in early 2007 and ended up at The Weather Channel in mid 2013 before I thought I was done with the corporate world and joined the Wikimedia Foudation. So humbled to serve.

My work

I hope to improve your experience on Wikipedia. I'm especially interested in humanizing the project.
