#VisibleWikiWomen

Wikimedia projects are still missing many images of important and influential women, especially black, brown, indigenous and trans women. #VisibleWikiWomen is a challenge to add more images of women to Commons and Wikipedia so that those who have been invisible can be fully seen! Join us from 8 March 2022 for the fifth edition of the campaign. Upload your contributions here!
