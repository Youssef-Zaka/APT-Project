Diversity, Equity and Inclusion Strategic Plan

Michigan State University released its Diversity, Equity and Inclusion Steering Committee Plan, culminating 18 months of review, stakeholder input and development. The plan was designed as a framework of recommendations to improve the culture around DEI and collaborate with overall strategic planning efforts across the university.
