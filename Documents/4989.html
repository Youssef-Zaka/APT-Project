Don’t just land a job,

build a career

After graduation, 84% of Merit America report a positive career outcome.

We know that succeeding the job market requires more than just technical skills. Our curriculum goes deep on the skills you need to stand out.

Professional skills are practiced throughout the Merit America program via coaching and squad sessions. Each squad session focuses on a specific professional skills topic like resume writing or feedback in the workplace.

There are even events like mock interviews to make sure you’re ready to land a job and build a career you’ll love.

At Merit America, you’re not alone. You’re matched with a professional career coach to help you be your best self. They’ll help you identify your strengths and weaknesses, and will keep you accountable when things get tough.

After you complete your technical learning, our expert team of Job Success Specialists help you apply for jobs that meet your specific interests and your skills. Our placement team also works directly with employers to find specific job opportunities for Merit America graduates.

Because of this, 84% of graduates report a positive career outcome after the program.
