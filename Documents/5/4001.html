2019-2020 China pneumonia outbreak, or China pnumonia, commonly known as Wuhan pnumia (Chinese: 武漢肺炎; pinyin: wǔhàn fèiyán) or pneumonia of unknown origin (Chinese: 不明原因肺炎; pinyin: bùmíng yuányīn fèiyán), is the pneumonia outbreak firstly discovered in Huanan Seafood Market in Wuhan, China. [1][2]
