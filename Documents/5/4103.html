Risotto is an Italian rice dish, most popular in the north of Italy. Historically, risotto is the name given to all recipes that use rice as the main component and are served as first dish in a traditional Italian lunch or dinner

More featured ingredients »
Cucumber is a green vegetable related to the squash and melon. Cucumbers have a high water content and are popular in salads and as pickles. Except in the preparation of some pickles, cucumbers are virtually never cooked.
