The GLAM–Wiki initiative ("galleries, libraries, archives, and museums" with Wikipedia; also including botanic gardens and zoos) helps cultural institutions share their resources with the world through collaborative projects with experienced Wikipedia editors.

  • See Wikimedia Outreach for GLAM outreach around the world
  • See Commons for tips on uploading images for use in GLAM articles
