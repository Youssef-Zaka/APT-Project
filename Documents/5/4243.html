Template documentation[view] [edit] [history] [purge]

This template is a helpbox, otherwise known as a help infobox. It is a navigational aid which is intended to guide Wikipedia editors to information about copyright on Wikipedia, both how to respect the copyright of rightsholders elsewhere, and how reusers can respect them and Wikipedian rightsholders. It is intended to be displayed on and be relevant to all Wikipedia namespace pages which have to do with copyright. Suggestions for additions and corrections to it can be made at Template talk:Wikipedia copyright.

Usage

{{Wikipedia copyright}} produces the helpbox on the above. No parameters are supported.
