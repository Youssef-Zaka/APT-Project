Shared Knowledge (Macedonian: Споделено знаење, transliterated: Spodeleno znaenje) is an organisation of Wikimedians established in March 2014 with the main goal to run mission-aligned initiatives and projects on the territory of Macedonia.

Membership[edit]

Regular member of the organisation can become any individual who is willing to take active part in the projects of the Association by filling out the membership application. Therewith the application it is necessary to submit a copy of ID card or other document for identification on the official address of the organisation, ul. Šidska 68a, Gjorče Petrov, Skopje.
