Usage

Although there are over two hundred Wikipedia guidelines, this sidebar contains the most foundational or frequently referenced, organized by the categories defined in Wikipedia:List of guidelines, some of which are headings and the others contained under the heading "Other". This sidebar is recommended for guidelines and other project pages at which navigation to other guidelines is an indicated feature.

The above documentation is transcluded from Template:Guideline list/doc. (edit | history)
Editors can experiment in this template's sandbox (edit | diff) and testcases (edit) pages.
