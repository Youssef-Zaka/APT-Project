SOCIAL JUSTICE

DEPTH

DEPTH

TRANSPARENCY

FOCUS

FOCUS

EXPERTISE

EXPERTISE

More than 50% of our searches are for CEOs or successors to founders; we also bring a deep track record recruiting CFOs, COOs, heads of Strategy, Evaluation/Learning, Marketing and Innovation for high-performing nonprofits, foundations, social enterprises and mission-driven businesses working in animal welfare, education, environment, global development, health/wellness, human services (child welfare, disability services, workforce development), and policy.

RELATIONSHIPS

RELATIONSHIPS
