Overview

On Wednesday, 15 January 2020, the Wikimedia Foundation started to receive reports that the block of Wikipedia in Turkey is being lifted, data which is also indicated by our internal traffic reports.

Turkey - July 2019 social media toolkit

Overview

On Monday, July 22nd, the Wikimedia Foundation announced some exciting news about our case against Turkeyâ€™s block on Wikipedia that is before the European Court of Human Rights (ECHR). The Court has selected the case for priority status.

This means our case is moving forward more quickly in the Court. This is important because a decision in our case may help set a precedent for how information is shared online. Â 

We would love to have the support of Wikimedia Foundation supporters in spreading the good news about this next step in our case, and more broadly, in reiterating the importance of supporting free knowledge around the world.

This toolkit contains social media captions and graphics to help you and your organization amplify this news. We hope youâ€™ll do so today or in the coming days and weeks.

Social media specifics
