Message from Mr Macky SALL, President of the Republic of Senegal

We are on the last steps towards the 9th World Water Forum, scheduled for 21-26 March 2022, under the theme of “Water Security for Peace and Development”.

Despite the Covid-19 pandemic, Senegal has remained steadfast in its willingness and commitment to host the world community in Dakar, in order to place water at the heart of multilateralism and international policies, to build effective response mechanisms to multifaceted crises and the construction of a resilient, prosperous and stable post-Covid-19 world.

The duty to change our world questions our ability to act, and in time! In the current context where global warming increases the risk of major sociopolitical crises while having adverse effects...
