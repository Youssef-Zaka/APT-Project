Our Network

WIRED Consulting draws on an unrivalled network of innovators, influencers, strategic thinkers and award-winning journalists. This unique network gives us the most credible, provocative and progressive picture of what’s shaping the future and why. As a result, we have consistently and correctly anticipated the impact of trends on businesses across the globe.
