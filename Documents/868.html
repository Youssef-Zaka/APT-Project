Condé Nast Britain

For more than 100 years, Condé Nast has been synonymous with engaging, visually arresting and innovative publishing.

Today, the world’s leading privately-owned multimedia company continues to create the finest magazine brands in the world, and leads the way in producing outstanding digital content across multiple platforms.

About Us

Iconic brands are shaped by people with passion, innovation and creativity.

More than 750 employees in Condé Nast Britain and at Condé Nast International’s London headquarters help drive the ongoing success of our expanding business.

To work at Condé Nast is to be a part of a renowned, influential and fast-evolving company dedicated to creating and distributing engaging, extraordinary content, across multiple platforms.

It is also to be part of a company investing in development and change, across a diverse portfolio - from the established print and website brands to our numerous new ventures such as video, events and education.

If you are looking for a rewarding and stimulating career, then Condé Nast is seeking talented and enthusiastic individuals to help shape the future of the business.

Equal Opportunities

Condé Nast is committed to a comprehensive policy of equal opportunities in employment where individuals are selected and treated on the basis of their relevant merits and abilities, without regard to age, disability, gender reassignment, marital or civil partner status, pregnancy or maternity, race, colour, nationality, ethnic or national origin, religion or belief, sex or sexual orientation (the protected characteristics).

Condé Nast does not discriminate because of any protected characteristic. Our policy and practice require that all employees are afforded equal opportunities within employment, and that entry into employment and career progression will be determined only by personal merit. In all cases, an individual’s ability to perform a role will be the primary consideration.

Advertising Sales Graduate Scheme

Our Advertising Sales Graduate Scheme can offer an
invaluable grounding for a successful career in the world of publishing.

Our graduates are given intensive, visionary training in sales techniques, copywriting, advertisement design and client management.

The Condé Nast Classified department is fast-paced and hugely successful, publishing the highest number of classified pages of any magazine group in the UK.

The department is responsible for all classified advertising pages across the Condé Nast portfolio. Our pages, far from the traditional idea of classified advertising, are engaging, innovative and tailored to each individual title and client. Our thriving sales team is made up of dynamic graduates who source and pitch to potential new clients, build on existing client relationships and create relevant and eye-catching advertising pages.

Our advertisers receive the very best in client care and a first-rate artwork service, resulting in longstanding contracts and revenue.

Our aim is to promote people from within, whenever possible. There are opportunities for outstanding sales executives to progress within the classified department to managerial level. In addition, a significant number of our classified team members have progressed into a wide variety of exciting positions within Condé Nast.

To apply for the Advertising Sales Graduate Scheme, please click the button below.

Condé Nast College of Fashion & Design

The Condé Nast College, operating from its state-of-the-art facility in the heart of Soho, provides students with a world-class fashion education. All of our courses fuse exciting and intensive academic learning with unrivalled access to the best and brightest minds in the industry, including the Condé Nast magazine teams at Vogue, LOVE, Glamour, GQ etc. While our courses are extremely creative, their purpose is not to teach our students how to be fashion designers – instead they learn about styling, journalism, buying, marketing, branding, graphic design, PR, digital strategy, business, events, entrepreneurship, e-commerce and the myriad other career paths into this exciting and fast-moving industry.

Temporary Pool

Join the Condé Nast temporary talent pool

We use a number of temporary staff to help cover holiday and long term sickness absences. If you would like do some casual work while looking for a permanent position or in the holidays from your studies, please do apply by following the link at the bottom of our current vacancies page.

Most temporary assignments are of an administrative nature so office-based skills, organisation and a polite telephone manner are essential. We often need to fill these positions on short notice so we really do appreciate candidate’s flexibility and enthusiasm. Successful candidates will be invited to an interview with a member of the Talent team and they will then be added to the temporary pool.
