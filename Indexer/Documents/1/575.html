The Suicide task force is a work group of WikiProject Death, set up to provide a coherent framework for development and organization of suicide-related articles.

Scope[edit]

This task force deals with suicide, include suicide rates, causes, philosophy, legislation, prevention, etc.

Resources on responsibly covering suicides[edit]

"Copycat suicides", or "suicide contagion", is when someone emulates another suicide they learned about either from local knowledge or due to accounts or depictions of the original suicide on television and in other media. Responsible and considerate coverage of suicides can decrease the risk of copycats.

  • Recommendations for Reporting on Suicide from ReportingonSuicide.org
  • BloggingonSuicide.org
  • The facts about safe reporting of suicide from The Conversation
  • Preventing Suicide: A Resource for Media Professionals from the World Health Organization
