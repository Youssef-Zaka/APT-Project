          Wikimedia Foundation Office Wiki

          This is the Wikimedia Foundation Office Wiki Intranet. It is not the same as:

          • board.wikimedia.org – that is for the Board, the Executive Director, and the General Counsel of the Wikimedia Foundation
          • wikimediafoundation.org – that is the public site about the Wikimedia Foundation
          • foundation.wikimedia.org – that is the public Wikimedia Foundation Governance Wiki
          • meta.wikimedia.org – that is the public wiki for all cross-project coordination
          • vrt-wiki.wikimedia.org – that is the private wiki for all VRT responders
          • collab.wikimedia.org – that is the private wiki for collaboration between the Wikimedia Foundation and external vendors
          • internal.wikimedia.org – that was the private wiki for trusted volunteers, staff and chapter organizations, now inactive

          This particular wiki is limited to:

          • Paid staff of the Wikimedia Foundation
          • Board members
