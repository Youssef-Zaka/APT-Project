Transform Technology Summits

Hear from CIOs, CTOs, and other C-level execs on data and AI strategies

Register for Transform

About the Summits

This Transform Summit series is a part of VentureBeat’s mission to be a town square for technical decision-makers to gain knowledge about transformative technology and transact. These c-level summits deliver information on data technologies and strategies to guide you as you lead your organizations. We invite you to be part of this series and to become a member of our community.

Lunch, Learn, Connect

Learn from F1000 brand CXO’s, who will share their experiences and insights on the topic of the day and walk the audience through the ‘wow’ and the ‘how’ of case studies of low-code/no-code implementations.

In-person networking returns with fireside chats with CXO’s from F1000 brands from various industry verticals, who will share their experience and expertise with the audience on the topic of the day.

Executive forum roundtables (brands-only), and various other virtual networking opportunities with executive-level attendees from end-user brands, software providers, media companies, and more.

Transform 2022

July 19 | The Data and AI Executive Summit | San Francisco, CA
July 20 – 22 | The Data Week | Virtual
July 26 – 28 | The AI Week | Virtual

The #1 publisher in AI coverage, VentureBeat, returns with their flagship event, Transform—NorCal’s leading event on applied AI for enterprise business and technology decision-makers.
 
During this event, industry experts will come together for a full week to provide comprehensive coverage across the breadth and depth of the world of applied AI, as well as industry-focused vertical tracks in healthcare, finance, retail, manufacturing, and security.
