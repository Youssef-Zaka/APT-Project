Publish more than the final paper

Studies have shown that articles with associated data published alongside receive an increase in citations.

Your authors want to get credit for all their work, including what publishers consider supplemental material. 

Your readers also expect more content, and context, than ever before.

Learn about the Portal, Workflows and Integrations, Preprints and Figshare & FAIR

Since 2011, Figshare has been making data (and a variety of other types of supplemental material) more discoverable and accessible to the broader academic and scholarly communities worldwide. We have done so in partnership with more than 20 major publishers, building an ever-growing set of now over four million outputs.

Pair this with the fact that studies show that journal articles receive more citations when their associated data is made openly available - and it becomes clear that publishing the minimal version of your authors’ research isn’t enough anymore.

Get in touch to learn how you can publish more than the final paper with Figshare.
