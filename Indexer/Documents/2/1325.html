Application Timelines

First-year Student Admission Timeline

Early Decision 2021-22
Submit all required application materials (application mailing instructions) November 1
Financial aid application materials due for international students November 1
Financial aid application materials due for U.S. citizens and eligible non-citizens November 21
Architecture and art portfolio and interview due Check Deadline
College of Human Ecology design supplements (for those applying to DEA and FD&M) Check Deadline
Admission decisions and financial aid awards announced Mid-December
Regular Decision 2021-22
Submit all required application materials (application mailing instructions) January 2
Financial aid application materials due for international students January 2
Financial aid application materials due for U.S. citizens and eligible non-citizens February 15
Architecture and art portfolio and interview due Check Deadline
College of Human Ecology design supplements (for those applying to DEA and FD&M) Check Deadline
Admission decisions and financial aid awards announced Early April
Reply to offer of admission May 1                         

Transfer Admission Timeline

Spring Semester Transfer Admission 2021-22
Submit all required application materials (application mailing instructions) October 15
Financial Aid application materials due for U.S. citizens and eligible non-citizens October 15
Architecture and art portfolio and interview due Check Deadline
College of Human Ecology design supplements (for those applying to DEA and FD&M) Check Deadline
Admission decisions and financial aid awards announced November
Reply to offer of admission Early January

*Please note The College of Engineering and The Charles H. Dyson School of Applied Economics and Management do not offer Spring Transfer Admission.

Fall Semester Transfer Admission 2021-22
Submit all required application materials (application mailing instructions) March 15
Financial Aid application materials due for international students March 15
Financial Aid application materials due for U.S. citizens and eligible non-citizens March 15
Architecture and art portfolio and interview due Check Deadline
College of Human Ecology design supplements (for those applying to DEA and FD&M) Check Deadline
Admission decisions and financial aid awards announced May and June
Reply to offer of admission 30 days from the date of the admission offer or July 1, whichever is sooner

*Deadlines are postmark deadlines.
