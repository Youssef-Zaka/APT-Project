Astronomy

On these pages you will find Springer’s journals, books and eBooks in all areas of astronomy, serving researchers, professionals, lecturers, students, amateurs and general readers. Our book and eBook portfolio comprises major reference works, textbooks, monographs, conference proceedings, and popular science books from the world’s most distinguished authors. Well-known publications include: Living Reviews in Relativity, The Astronomy and Astrophysics Review.
