Rgaudin (rɛg) is the user account of renaud gaudin.

Projects I'm involved in (at various levels)[edit]

  • Kunnafoni, Not-for-profit organization working for a better Access to Information in Mali and elsewhere.
  • Ubuntu mali, Local Community promoting Free Software and GNU/Linux in Mali.
  • Kiwix, Offline Wikimedia distribution.
  • MALeBOOKS, eReaders filled with free eBooks and Wikipedia for Mali.
  • Jokkolabs Bamako, coworking and social change hub in Mali.
  • Wikimédia France, French Wikimedia chapter.
  • Wikimedia CH, Swiss Wikimedia chapter.
  • One Laptop Per Child.
