Looking for Data Management Resources?

This LibGuide contains resources for learning about data management and implementing data management techniques into your research workflow. Using the tabs on the left side of the screen, you can navigate across different data management topics and links to helpful external resources. If you would like one-on-one or group/lab-wide support for data management, please email us at data@cmu.libanswers.com or contact us through our webform!
