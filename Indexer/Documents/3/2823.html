Researchers

Explore connections to deliver new and unique insights

As a researcher, being able to find relevant information fast is crucial in your daily work. With Dimensions, you have free access to a vast collection of curated research outputs and tools that help you source exactly what you need to advance your own discoveries.

Instant full-text access

With just a single click, you can access the full text of millions of Open Access articles. If your university subscribes to Dimensions, the publications they license may also be accessible with a single click.

Full text search and powerful filtering

Dimensions full-text search offers a breadth of search results across journals and books. Our rich filtering options provide you with a powerful way to quickly curate search results to suit your specific needs.

Linked information at your fingertips

Want to know more about which patents and clinical trials are linked to a particular publication? Or discover who has cited or used your research? Dimensions’ linked database of grants, patents, datasets and clinical trials, along with its up-to-the-minute citation and Altmetric attention data, means you can view information in context.

Explore rich researcher profiles

Our researcher pages, aggregated using advanced machine learning techniques, display the outputs of individual researchers, from publications to patents.

Connection to ORCiD

Link your ORCiD account with Dimensions and you can add publications from Dimensions to your ORCiD record. An up-to-date publications list helps to make your research more discoverable and ensure you receive credit for your full body of work.

We’d be happy to provide more information on how Dimensions can support your organization – please contact us and we will be in touch
