Dimensions for Pharma

Innovate your scientific research process and beat information overload

Providing the most comprehensive view of the whole research landscape, Dimensions supports the discovery and development of future drugs and delivers the insights you need.

It’s the only service that extracts data across the full research lifecycle, with more data than any other provider.

You can speed up your research process and generate more accurate insights than ever before – because Dimensions uses AI-powered semantic search that incorporates ontologies of 40 million concepts with 100 million synonyms.

This is a breadth and depth of data that you can’t find anywhere else – making sure that you don’t miss a thing.

“Dimensions takes scientific knowledge discovery and insight generation to the next level.”
DR. KARLHEINZ SPENNY, HEAD OF SCIENTIFIC INFORMATION CENTER , BOEHRINGER INGELHEIM PHARMA GMBH & CO KG

Game-changing advantages

Find more of what you need fast

We leave no stone unturned. Even complex queries deliver results in seconds.

You can explore across the whole research lifecycle, from over 120 million publications to data on grants, clinical trials, patents, datasets and policy documents.

And, with over 70% of publications available with full text indexing, you will discover more relevant information than anywhere else.

You can be confident in your searches, as Dimensions finds up to 10 times more publications than PubMed.

Miss nothing

Rapidly uncover unexpected insights in real time with co-occurence analysis and semantic ontologies that find data overlooked by other tools.

Predict patterns and trends easily to innovate at speed.
Explore drug, target and disease landscapes and easily spot gaps for development; and create powerful visualizations from high-quality, connected datasets.

Minimize error

Make accurate, evidence-based decisions with confidence; mitigate risk; and increase efficacy.

Our customized dashboards ensure you can see all critical information at a glance. Take a look at this oncology biomarkers dashboard.

Accelerate your route to market

Lessen the risk of failure at a clinical trial as you speed up the research process, and easily spot gaps for commercial development.

Get to market faster so your work can start improving people’s lives.
