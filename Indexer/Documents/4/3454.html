An Introduction to Britannica Books

ENGAGED LEARNING

Our beautiful books combine cutting-edge information with a playful approach to learning, encouraging kids to draw connections between topics and discover awe-inspiring facts about our world.

hand-drawn illustrations

Stunning original artwork features in all of our titles, enriching knowledge, and adding warmth and wit to each subject.

EXPERT INSPIRATION

At Britannica Books, there is an expert behind every page!

 All the information is thoroughly fact-checked by the team at Encyclopaedia Britannica. Specialist experts often feature in our pages, too––so kids will be inspired by the people who are making discoveries today.
