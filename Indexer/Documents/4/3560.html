How it works

Connect with another school and find English-speaking pen pals to start exchanging cards with their students. We believe learning English should be an exhilarating experience. So far, we’ve helped thousands of students improve their English and feel more confident communicating in a practical way. With over 16,000 schools already enjoying the benefits of having international penfriends, get started right away by registering your school.

1. Register your school

Register your school today, telling us where you are, how many students are taking part and the age group of your students.

2. Find a partner school

Look at the world map on our website to find language pen pals in another country. Find international pen friends from schools that are like your own in terms of age range and number of penfriends.

3. Upload cards

Choose 30 of your students’ penpal cards, scan or photograph them, and upload them to our website for the world to see.
