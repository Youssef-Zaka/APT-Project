Combining Distant and Direct Supervision for Neural Relation Extraction

@inproceedings{Beltagy2019CombiningDA,

  • Iz Beltagy, Kyle Lo, Waleed Ammar
  • Published in NAACL 30 October 2018
  • Computer Science
In relation extraction with distant supervision, noisy labels make it difficult to train quality models. [] Key Method We improve such models by combining the distant supervision data with an additional directly-supervised data, which we use as supervision for the attention weights. We find that joint training on both types of supervision leads to a better model because it improves the model's ability to identify noisy sentences.
