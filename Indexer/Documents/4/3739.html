GWeb Sign-on

Sign in using the appropriate button below.

Current students, faculty, staff, and alumni as of 2019: Log into the GWeb Information System using your GW email address ([email protected]) and password.

 

Former Students: 

If you are a former student (currently not a student or have not graduated from GW within the past year), click the Current Students button to access GWeb with your GW Email (NetID & Password). If it has been more than one year since you were a former student, click the Applicants button to access GWeb with your GWID & Pin.

Need to make updates to your two-step authentication methods?

You can update your two-step authentication methods by signing into Microsoft Authenticator Security Info, or visit our two-step authentication page for more information, including detailed instructions on how to modify your authentication methods.

 

Access with GW Email

  • Current Students
  • Current Faculty & Staff
  • Alumni 2019 and after

Access with GWID & PIN

  • Applicants
  • Former Faculty & Staff
  • Alumni prior to 2019
