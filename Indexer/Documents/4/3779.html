Visit

MSU is one of the biggest, greenest campuses in the country, welcoming visitors from around the world.

We invite you to explore our amazing campus, but before you do, visit our Together We Will website to learn how we're keeping Spartans and visitors safe, including requiring face coverings, physical distancing and more.

Visit options

There’s much to see and do on MSU’s 5,300-acre campus. Come visit, and take a look around. Be sure to view our campus visitor guidelines. 

Are you a prospective student? Check out the in-person and virtual tours that the Office of Admissions offers.
