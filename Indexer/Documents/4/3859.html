Implementation

Meteorite deliver effective data analysis by anticipating the needs of the users and considering the broader requirements of the organisation at the start of the project. By understanding the data sources, data quality, performance and security requirements, we deliver solutions that are accurate, robust and scalable. For any Saiku project to be a success it is crucial that the user can access the data they need quickly, in terms they understand and can have confidence in the numbers. Our implementation services cover Saiku and all of the processes and technologies required to support Saiku, from architecture and system design to data storage, integration and quality.

Embedded Analytics

Saiku is the perfect tool for adding reporting, data discovery and self service analytics to applications and websites. Saiku can be quickly deployed to provide ad hoc query and analysis to any application or website. The intuitive drag and drop interface allows any user to ask any question of the data via the browser. Meteorite help many of our customers to extend the functionality of their application or website and offer their users flexible, secure reporting, visualisation and analysis. Saiku offers a wide range of customisation options from a completely custom front to selectable functionality and integrated look and feel to match the customer application. Meteorite can also provide security integration and single sign to provide completely seamless access.

Bespoke Requirements

For customers who have unique or unusual requirements Meteorite are able to develop new functionality or extend existing functionality of Saiku to meet the exact requirements of the customer. Extensions can include anything from integration of unusual data sources, custom visualisations or providing solutions to non-standard use cases. If you would like to discuss your requirements, please get in touch.
