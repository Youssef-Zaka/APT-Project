The following is a list of all usernames currently in use on Wikipedia, or those in a specified group. A starting name can be specified. If you are a new user seeking to create an account on Wikipedia, please make sure your desired name is not displayed after searching for it below. See also: Wikipedia:Username policy.

If a user account has some local group memberships, they will be displayed beside their names within parentheses. The assignment and revocation of most of these roles is logged at Special:Log/rights; some changes, particularly removal of bureaucrat access and all changes to CheckUser and oversight access, are instead carried out by stewards and are logged at m:Special:Log/rights; changes affecting the English Wikipedia show up with "username@enwiki" instead of just the username. Please note that certain individuals may have global rights - see Special:GlobalUsers or Special:CentralAuth to check the global rights of a user. Others may not be actively flagged (due to inactivity, a desire to temporarily step away from the project, etc.) but still retain the rights in name. The automatically assigned autoconfirmed user right is not displayed here but can be seen at Special:UserRights.

For a list of users who have recently been active, see Special:ActiveUsers.
