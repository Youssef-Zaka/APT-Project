  • Students can access their apps, settings, and more from any device

Easy to use

Deploy and manage simply

  • Easy to distribute 1 or 100 devices

  • Simple to manage thousands of devices and set 200+ policies and commands

  • Add users, devices, printers, and internet from central Google Admin Console

Flexible

Accessible for all learners

  • Centrally manage hundreds of thousands of devices from Admin Console

  • Versatile form factors to suit different budgets and needs

  • Shareable devices can be used across multiple classrooms and students

Secure

Get the latest security updates

  • Background updates install the latest security and features every 6 weeks

  • Two versions of Chrome OS on every device

  • Sandboxing of identities, user profiles, and OS itself

  • Layers of data encryption and verified security checks on boot up

Dr. Lessell Martiny Bray, Chief Technology and Information Officer, Forsyth County Georgia

Case Studies

Learn how schools across the country are using Chromebooks

  • Fairfield County School District

    The Fairfield County School District in South Carolina has seen an increase in student engagement and double digit growth in test scores with the help of Chromebooks.

  • Carroll School

    Margaret Kuzmicz, Director of Technology at Carroll School, uses Chromebooks touch screen devices at her school to make learning more accessible for students with language-based disabilities and beyond.

  • Maine Township High School District

    Test scores are consistently higher than the national average at Maine Township High School District 207 in Chicago, where every student has access to a Chromebook to use at school and at home.
