Open Rights Group (ORG) is a non-profit organisation that relies on people like you who care about protecting basic rights in the digital age.

Your contributions enable us to challenge the Government’s mass surveillance programme and stand up to powerful tech giants that abuse your personal data to control what you see and say online.
