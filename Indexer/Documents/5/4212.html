    Benoît Evellin
    Community Relations Specialist, Wikimedia Foundation
    "Give a man a fish and you feed him for a day; teach a man to fish and you feed him for a lifetime."

    About me

    See this page in French

    You may know me in the Wikimedia movement as Trizek. I've started editing French Wikipedia in May 2008, and after multiple adventures, I'm now involved in helping newbies in their first steps.

    When I'm not doing stuff about Wikimedia, I like to play folk music, practice model railroading, stage and screen combat (fencing), role-playing games and more.

    I live in Brittany, France.

    My work

    I've joined the Wikimedia Foundation in May 2015, to be one of the Community Relations Specialists. I'm now working part time (Mondays, Tuesdays, Wednesdays).

    As of June 2021, I'm assisting the Growth team. Up to December 31, 2021, I'm a Global interface editor in order to help Growth features deployments.

    I discuss with readers, editors, volunteer developers and Wikimedia Foundation's staff so that they can know each other, know what they all do, what they all expect and what they all hope for. I create and facilitate discussions, with a focus on newcomers.

    I'm also working on documentation and easing translations or anything else that requires my support. See my assigned tasks.

    I may not be able to communicate in your language for this mission and I'm sorry about that. Please help me to help you!

    Disclaimer: I work for or provide services to the Wikimedia Foundation, and this is the account I try to use for edits or statements I make in that role. However, the Foundation does not vet all my activity, so edits, statements, or other contributions made by this account may not reflect the views of the Foundation.
