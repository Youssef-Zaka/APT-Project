Youth Standing up for Human Rights[edit]

Scope[edit]

The scope of this list is topics within the special theme of the UN this year: Youth Standing Up for Human Rights.

For this campaign this means increasing coverage of:

  • Youth Activists or human rights defenders who advocate for rights
  • Youth movements or organizations widely acknowledged for their focus on a particular right
  • General topics related to youth rights, the cultural, political or international context of advocating for those rights, etc.

This does not include:

  • Violations or violators of youth rights
