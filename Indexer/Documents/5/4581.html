Fact or Fiction: Verifying Scientific Claims

@article{Wadden2020FactOF,
  title={Fact or Fiction: Verifying Scientific Claims},

  • David Wadden, Kyle Lo, Hannaneh Hajishirzi
  • Published 30 April 2020
  • Computer Science
  • ArXiv
We introduce scientific claim verification, a new task to select abstracts from the research literature containing evidence that supports or refutes a given scientific claim, and to identify rationales justifying each decision. To study this task, we construct SciFact, a dataset of 1.4K expert-written scientific claims paired with evidence-containing abstracts annotated with labels and rationales.
