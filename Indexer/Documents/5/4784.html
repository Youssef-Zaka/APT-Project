Ukraine's Cultural Diplomacy Month is an article writing contest focused on improving the coverage of Ukrainian culture on Wikipedia in different languages, organized by Wikimedia Ukraine in cooperation with Ukrainian Institute and Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Ukraine.

The idea of Ukraine's Cultural Diplomacy Month is to accumulate and distribute information about the culture and famous people of Ukraine in as many language editions of Wikipedia as possible in order to increase the quantity and quality of free knowledge available about Ukraine globally. Wikipedia is missing articles about all kinds of topics, and through this contest, participants can improve articles about cultural phenomena important to Ukrainian people in any language.
