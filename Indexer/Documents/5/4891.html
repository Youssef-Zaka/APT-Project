Social Responsibility

Photo by Kathy Ryan

Beyond our journalism, we are committed to helping people through educational initiatives, and giving back to the community through college scholarships and financial assistance to those in need.

The New York Times Neediest Cases Fund

For more than 100 years, The New York Times Neediest Cases Fund has supported people in need through generous donations from our readers that are distributed by organizations that provide direct assistance.

Learn more about the Neediest Cases Fund

Educational Initiatives

The Times Company supports a number of education initiatives that teach students about the world and the news media from a young age into adulthood.

Learn more about our educational initiatives

The New York Times College Scholarship Program

The New York Times College Scholarship Program provides four-year scholarships, mentoring and internship opportunities for recipients.

Learn more about the College Scholarship Program
