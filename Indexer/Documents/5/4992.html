How the USO is Supporting Troops Deployed to Eastern Europe and Their Families - Learn More

War in Ukraine

Tensions continue to rise between Russia and Ukraine and thousands of U.S. troops have been deployed throughout Eastern Europe. Learn more about how the USO is always by their side.

What does it take to keep our service members strong?

It takes a force. Their journey is everyone's responsibility. From the moment they enlist, through their assignments and deployments, and as they transition back to their communities, they can rely on your support.

San Antonio, Texas

USO helps make enlistment easier - Read More

When they enlist

From the moment recruits swear an oath to military service, the USO stands behind them as they volunteer to serve our country.

  • USO volunteers welcome new recruits at USO locations inside Military Entrance Processing Stations (MEPS).

  • Introduce new recruits and their families to the wide range of USO support offered around the world.

  • Provide a friendly, relaxing environment as they begin their military careers as soldiers, sailors, airmen, Marines and Coast Guardsmen.

Southwest Asia

Since 2002, USO has supported troops in Afghanistan - Read More

On the front lines

When troops are ordered into combat zones, the USO provides a home away from home to help bridge the distance.

  • Deliver the comforts of home through care packages, snacks, and refreshments.

  • Provide entertainment by supporting celebrity USO tours, movies and video games.

  • Connect service members with phone calls home and free Wi-Fi access inside our centers.

Fayetteville, North Carolina

One Family’s Lifelong Journey With the USO - Read More

With their families

The USO keeps military families strong, no matter where their service may take them.

  • Connect soldiers and their families with phone calls home and video chats.

  • Strengthen communities with military spouse networking, family meals and activities for kids.

  • Provide wellness activities with family workshops, gaming and fitness programming.

Kandahar, Afganistan

USO centers are a home away from home - Read More

Away from home

We’re always by the side of our service members – no matter where they are – with our global network of USO centers, programs and volunteers.

  • Programs to build stronger relationships with home and each other.

  • Holiday Care Packages, delivered for winter, spring, summer and fall, feature seasonal items to help troops celebrate American holidays.

  • State-of-the-art Mobile USO vehicles offer service members the same support the USO provides in an airport or installation location.

Bethesda, Maryland

USO is a kind face in the darkest hour - Read More

In their hour of need

When war takes a physical toll, the USO is there to offer comfort and support to wounded, ill and injured troops and their families.

  • Provide information, programming and community support at two warrior centers near Washington, D.C., and another in Landstuhl, Germany.

  • When a service member makes the ultimate sacrifice, the USO embraces loved ones at every step of their journey to Dover Air Force Base, Delaware.

  • Support military families through partnerships with military veteran organizations.

Tacoma, Washington

One-on-one support during transition

Transitioning home

When military service to the nation is complete, the USO helps service members and their families successfully transition into civilian life.

  • Develop a clear plan for the future as they transition out of the military.

  • Offer one-on-one support and access to local community networks and resources to help soldiers, sailors, airmen, Marines and Coast Guardsmen reach personal and professional goals.

  • Host summits, workshops and networking events for transitioning service members and military spouses.
