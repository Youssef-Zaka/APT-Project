    Benefits

    Easily and securely enable developers to build FHIR API-based digital services

    Reduce risks during care transitions

    Improve the efficiency and safety of transitions across the continuum of in-patient and out-patient care.

    Deliver patient-centric digital services

    Provide targeted personal wellness and prevention guidance to at-risk participants.

    Improve chronic condition management

    Empower collaboration between patients, physicians, and healthcare providers to effectively manage chronic conditions.

    Key features

    HealthAPIx enables Payers, Providers, and Life Science organizations to innovate

    FHIR server-agnostic platform

    Manage, secure, and scale APIs with an enterprise-grade platform that is FHIR-server agnostic.
    Ingest data from anywhere

    Easily ingest healthcare data from internal, external, or open source FHIR-ready partners.
    Innovate with ease

    Deliver on the promise of patient-centric healthcare and data interoperability by quickly launching digital services like new mobile apps and making apps and data sharing secure and compliant.
