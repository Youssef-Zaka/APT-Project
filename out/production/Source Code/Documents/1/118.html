Electrical engineering

Books in this subject area deal with electrical engineering: a field of engineering that deals with the study and application of electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism.

Electrical engineering is an extremely broad discipline, and the fields covered by the books in the area have a great deal of overlap. See the descriptions subpage for a list of books by description.
