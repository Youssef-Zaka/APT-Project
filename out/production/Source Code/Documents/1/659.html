Summary[edit]

DescriptionCensored section of Green Illusions by Ozzie Zehner.jpg
English: One of two censored sections of the 2012 environmental book Green Illusions by Ozzie Zehner. State laws forced the University of Nebraska Press to black-out sections of the book to be sold in the United States. "Veggie libel laws" enacted in 13 states make it illegal for journalists and authors to criticize the food industry.
