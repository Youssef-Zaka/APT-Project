The Training Modules are now available on the Outreach Dashboard! We need your help to translate these modules into your language. Help with translations!

Training modules are multilingual modules hosted on the Programs and Events Dashboard, operated by the Wiki Education Foundation.

One of the Wikimedia Foundation's Support and Safety team's annual plan goals in 2016-17 was to "introduce new, translatable, online training modules to help functionaries and affiliates better manage issues". For this previous work on these modules, refer to Training modules/Goals.
