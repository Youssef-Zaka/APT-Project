GamesBeat Summit:
Into the Metaverse 3

The intersection of science fiction, tech, and games

January 2023

View 2022 Sessions Here

Designed for Gaming Leaders Who are Diving Into the Metaverse Theory

GamesBeat Summit: Into the Metaverse 3 will be a two-day virtual event focused on the findings and emerging trends within the metaverse. Thought leaders will discuss the various factors that have impacted the latest changes and innovation, and how that may affect the future of the industry such as:

New avenues accessible to the metaverse
A possible decentralized and uncontrolled environment
Potential governance of commerce through the metaverse.

GamesBeat is looking for the best of the best speakers and sponsors. If you feel up to the challenge, please apply below!

Topics to be Covered

The Metaverse Ecosystem

New opportunities in 5G, cloud, infrastructure, human interfaces, and spatial computing

Communities and Commerce

Exploring the creator economy, social media platforms, and marketplaces

The Technology of the Metaverse

From science fiction inspiration to dealmaking, and financial trends
