FAQs
What is Stadia?

Stadia is Google’s gaming platform that lets you instantly play video games on compatible screens you already own. Stream games directly to your favorite compatible devices. Games are ready wherever you have internet, without waiting for installs, downloads, or updates⁵.
How does Stadia work?

Similar to the way you already stream TV shows or music, Stadia streams your games directly to your desktop, laptop, compatible phone or tablet. To play on TVs, grab Stadia Premiere Edition so you can play using a Stadia Controller and Google Chromecast Ultra⁶. With Stadia, you’re ready to play in seconds.

What is Stadia Pro?

Stadia Pro is a subscription that unlocks a growing collection of games to play on Stadia. Try Stadia today and you’ll start with a one month trial of Stadia Pro, including an assortment of games you can play right now (automatically $9.99/mo¹, cancel anytime². Subscription auto renews unless cancelled. Additional terms apply. Visit g.co/stadia/subscriptions to manage your subscription). Build a library of games that you can access as long as you’re a Stadia Pro subscriber. More games will be added to the Stadia Pro library for you to claim every month. You’ll also get other benefits such as monthly discounts.
How much does Stadia cost?


Stadia Pro is available to try for one month. Jump in with instant access to a growing collection of games (charged automatically at $9.99/mo¹ after trial, cancel anytime². Subscription auto renews unless cancelled. Visit g.co/stadia/subscriptions).

You can also buy games individually in the Stadia store even if you don’t have an active Stadia Pro subscription.

How do I play on Stadia?


You can play Stadia on your computer using the latest Chrome browser or your compatible Android phone/tablet using the Stadia app.

All you need is a keyboard and mouse on your computer to start playing. If you have a favorite controller, many of them are compatible with Stadia on computers and Android devices via a USB cable or Bluetooth, see here for a list of compatible controllers and gamepads.

To play on TVs, you need a Stadia Controller and Google Chromecast Ultra⁶.

How do I start playing a game on Stadia?

Go to Stadia.com on your computer or open the Stadia app on a supported phone. Then, go to the Stadia store and purchase a game to play. If you have a Stadia Pro subscription, claim one of the Stadia Pro games at no additional cost. After that, go to Home and just click the play button on the game you want to play.
What kind of internet do I need?


An internet connection speed of 10 megabits per second (Mbps) or greater is the minimum recommended to use Stadia.
