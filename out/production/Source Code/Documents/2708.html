Please read this deposit agreement (“Deposit Agreement”) carefully. By depositing research data and materials to and/or publishing with Singapore Management University Research Data Repository (SMU RDR), you represent that you are a current SMU faculty, staff or student, and you agree to the terms of this deposit agreement.

Definition. As used in this Deposit Agreement, “Provided Materials” means data, figure, computer code, images, software, and any other kind of research-related materials that you deposit in SMU RDR.

Eligibility to Deposit Provided Materials. You must be a SMU faculty, staff member, or student with an authorized SMU account, or be invited by a SMU faculty, staff member, or student to a project in order to deposit Materials in SMU RDR.
