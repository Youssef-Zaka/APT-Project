mechanics

From Wiktionary
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Noun[change]

Singular
mechanic

Plural
mechanics

  1. (plural only); (physics) The part of physics that studies the action of forces on objects.
  2. (plural only) The structure or details of something.
    Bob understands the mechanics of baseball better than me.
  3. The plural form of mechanic; more than one (kind of) mechanic.
