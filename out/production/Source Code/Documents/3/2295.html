Cambridge Pathway

Cambridge Primary

5 to 11 years old*

A broad and balanced curriculum:

  • 10 subjects (including English, Mathematics and Science)
  • Classroom and external assessments

Cambridge Lower Secondary

11 to 14 years old*

A broad and balanced curriculum:

  • 10 subjects (including English, Mathematics and Science)
  • Classroom and external assessments

Cambridge Upper Secondary

14 to 16 years old*

A wide choice of subjects at:

  • Cambridge IGCSE
    (70+ subjects)
  • Cambridge O Level
    (40+ subjects)

  • Cambridge ICE Certificate

Cambridge Advanced

16 to 19 years old*

A wide choice of subjects at:

  • Cambridge International AS & A Level (55+ subjects)
  • Cambridge Pre-U
    (20+ subjects)

  • Cambridge AICE Diploma
  • Cambridge IPQ

Cambridge CEM baseline assessments to measure potential and progress
