A fair, free and open future.

Our mission is to create a more open world – a world where all non-personal information is open, free for everyone to use, build on and share; and creators and innovators are fairly recognised and rewarded.

 

What we do

Working with individuals and organisations, we teach, create and inspire change.

Frictionless Data

Frictionless Data is a project that aims to dramatically increase the productivity of data, to create more insight, more efficiently.
