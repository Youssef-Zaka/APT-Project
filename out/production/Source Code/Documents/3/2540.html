      Wikimedia Research/Office hours

      From mediawiki.org
      < Wikimedia Research

      The Research team at Wikimedia Foundation holds office hours on the first Tuesday of each month (see below for exact date and time), which you can join via video call.

      The aim is to make ourselves more available to answer some of the research questions that you as Wikimedia volunteers, affiliates, staff, and researchers face in your projects and initiatives, and to connect, share, and learn from each other.

      Come and talk to us about anything related to Wiki-research, bring your questions and ideas to discuss projects, data, analysis, etc; for example:

      • You want to learn more about our team and our projects
      • You would like to share some research project or idea and would like to get feedback or support during office hours
      • You have questions about how to use specific datasets


      After initiating the office hours in January 2020, the current format using video-call is an experiment in order to facilitate more direct interaction. We would be glad to hear your feedback and/or comments on the talk-page.

      How it works[edit]

      Our office hours are held (usually) on the first Tuesday of each month and last 1 hour.

      You can join the videochat via this meeting link: https://meet.jit.si/WMF-Research-Office-Hours

      • We will have at least 2 members from the Research team be present at each office hour for discussion

      We will collect your topics to discuss in this etherpad: https://etherpad.wikimedia.org/p/Research-Analytics-Office-hours

      • If you have a specific item you would like to discuss or bring up, simply add it (together with your name)

      During the office hour:

      • We will take the first few minutes for introduction and organizing the agenda for the office hour
      • We then try to discuss each of the items on the agenda
        • Presentation of item/topic
        • Questions/comments according to the queue in the chat:
          • type o/ to raise your hand
          • type o// for direct response (use sparingly)
          • type o_ to lower your hand

      Additional information[edit]

      Announcements will be made over the following places, so subscribe to them if you want to receive the latest updates:

      • wiki-research-l mailing list
      • analytics mailing list
      • wikidata mailing list
      • wikimedia-l mailing list

      For specific questions related to Wikidata, see also the online office hours over at Wikidata

      If you have further questions, comments, or suggestions, please leave a comment on the talk-page or reach out to User:ELescak_(WMF).
