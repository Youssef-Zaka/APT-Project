Collecting Site Visitor Information (including use of Cookies and Web Beacons)

A cookie is a small text file containing information that a web site transfers to your computer for recordkeeping purposes. Cookies are used for running the Services, and are used to track you within the Services.

A web beacon is a transparent, 1-pixel image in .gif format that helps us collect data about visitors viewing a web page -- for example, to count the number of visitors to a page or to deliver a cookie to visitors' browsers when they view that page. We may use web beacons on the Services from time to time for these and other purposes.

When you visit the Services or click on internal links within the Services, we may collect information through technologies such as cookies and beacons.  By using the Services, you consent to any such collection and use of this information as described in this Privacy Statement.

We may use the information we gather to, among other things, help us improve our understanding of how visitors use the Services, measure interest in various pages, improve the Services’ content and usability, monitor the level of activity on the Services, and run the Services more efficiently.
