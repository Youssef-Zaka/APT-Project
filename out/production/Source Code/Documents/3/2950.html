Introduction

Wikimedia Community User Group Uganda is a team of self-motivated individuals who improve articles on Wikimedia Foundation project websites and promote free and open access to knowledge locally. They also invested in projects that sensitize and train communities about Wikipedia.

Objectives

  • To represent the Wikimedia Foundation to the Uganda Wikimedia contributing community
  • To promote Wikimedia projects and free content in general and to encourage the use of Wikimedia resources in education.
  • To recruit technically and non-technically minded people to participate to Wikimedia projects.
  • Organisation of conventions, conferences, meetings, workshops, contests, etc.

Projects and Activities

The Wikimedia Community User Group Uganda has organized and participated in activities that align with the Wikimedia vision and mission. These include:

  • Luganda Wikipedia For Education 2021
  • Luganda Wikipedia For Education 2020
  • Wikisoccerthons - 2020 pilot
  • Wikimedia 2030 Movement Strategy Salon Uganda
  • Initiation phase 2019, example: OpenStreetMap and Wikimedia activities
  • Wiki Loves Monuments 2018 Uganda
  • Wiki Loves Women 2018 - 2019
  • Wikidata Event Kampala(May 2018)
  • Art + Feminism Kampala (April 2018)
  • WikiGap 2018
