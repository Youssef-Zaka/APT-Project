    Get the bigger picture

    Introducing the Samsung Chromebook Plus V2 (LTE)

    With built-in LTE, the lightweight 2-in-1 Chromebook Plus lets you get more done anywhere, anytime, without having to search for Wi-Fi. Stay on top of projects with the included pen, dual camera, and full Google experience. Cross more off your list without slowing down with a powerful battery and Intel processor.

    Wach Film

    From laptop to tablet to sketchbook and more

    Weighing slightly over 3 pounds, the Chromebook Plus is light enough to slip into your bag and take anywhere. Easily fold it into tablet mode to switch between typing and writing down ideas.

    Always connected

    No Wi-Fi? No problem, thanks to LTE. Access the cloud and Google Apps anytime, anywhere. Instantly upload photos and videos to social media, or chat with loved ones via Hangouts.

    The power of the pen

    Experience the power of the precise built-in pen thats ready when you need it and never needs charging. Write a note, sketch an idea, edit a document, magnify and take screenshots.

    Two cameras, unlimited views

    Change your perspective with the 13MP world-facing camera with autofocus. Fold your laptop into tablet mode and capture your view from any angle or zoom in to take photos. Use the front camera to video chat. Store photos and videos right on your cloud.

    Get the most out of Google

    Take your experiences to the big screen. Navigate intuitively with the keyboard and touchpad. Easily download your favorite apps, movies and games from Google Play.

    Do more. Store more.

    Tackle anything with a long-lasting battery and an Intel Celeron processor that lets you work on multiple things at once. Store more with 32GB of built-in memory and expand your storage with a microSD card.*
