Welcome

Welcome

The University Privacy team provides guidance and resources to help navigate Cornell's regulatory obligations and ethical principles pertaining to the curation and use of data. Our team is focused on upholding Cornell's core values, facilitating solutions, enhancing a culture of compliance and ethics, and balancing compliance with mission-driven activities. We work closely with Cornell Information Technology to realize these goals in collaboration with units, colleges, and departments across campus.

University Privacy staff are dedicated to supporting Cornell's mission of learning, discovery, and engagement.
