Exploring new OED entries

Last updated: 09/01/2017
Contributor: Babette
Main Subject
English
Key stage
KS5
Category
Diversity and change: Language change
Resource type
Student activity
A selection of recent word entries to the Oxford English dictionary in 2014, with questions for students to explore some of the reasons these words have been coined. Includes differentiated hints to support lower attaining or less confident students. The words selected include vape, selfie and vlogger.
