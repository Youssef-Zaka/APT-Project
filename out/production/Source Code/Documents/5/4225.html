† sammyfoozle, v.

falsefalse

$Revision$
Origin: Formed within English, by compounding. Etymons: Sammy n., foozle v.
Etymology: < Sammy n. + foozle v.(Show Less)
Obsolete. rare.
Thesaurus »
Categories »
 

  transitive. To make a fool of; to cheat, con.

1837   E. Howard Old Commodore II. iii. 54   You have been sammyfoozled by a rascally swindler.

1837—1837(Hide quotations)
