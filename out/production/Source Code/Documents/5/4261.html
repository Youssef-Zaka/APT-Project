Activate analysts

By baking their knowledge of the data logic into LookML, analysts get the information key aspects of your data out of their head and into code. With self-serve data available, analysts are unbound from reactive reporting and free to actually analyze data.

Access accuracy

People will make decisions with or without the right data. Decisions based on inaccurate calculations or outdated numbers can be costly. Looker gives every employee access to the right data, defined the right way, at the right time.
