Magazine Style Design

ColorMag comes with beautiful and well thought magazine style design that will make your blog or magazine site stand out and gain more visitors.

WooCommerce Compatible

Need to setup online Store? This theme is fully compatible with WooCommerce plugin. Install the plugin, add products, setup payment methods and your are ready to sell.

Category Color Option

Represent your each category with unique color of your choice. This helps in creating beautiful colorful engaging magazine site.

Advanced Typography

Integrates more than 600 plus google fonts. Choose one that fits your site type. Also, change font size of almost every elements with simple clicks.

Primary Color Option

Choose one color that suits your theme brand or logo. This unique color will be reflected in buttons, menus and links etc.

Unique Posts System

This option helps you to show unique posts by removing the duplicate posts in major theme widget areas. One checkbox option and you are all set.

RTL Support

If your language is one of those which is written from Right to Left. No worries at all, there is built-in RTL support in this theme.

Multiple Color Option

Allows you to easily change the color of almost each and every bit of your site without getting your hand dirty with code.

15+ Widget Areas

Widget areas are available on important areas of the theme from header to footer. Add widgets of your choice to these widget areas.

6+ Custom Widgets

Free version contains 6+ custom widgets, specially targeted in creating professional looking magazine site. Pro comes with more additional widgets.

Search Engine Optimization

Follows best SEO practices. Title tags are wisely used so as to deliver your important content to search engines.

Major Browsers Compatible

Theme is tested with all major browsers. The site looks smooth & nice irrespective of the browser that you view your site on.

Boxed & Wide Layout

Choose the boxed or wide layout as you like. This will reflect in whole site. Boxed layout supports background color and backgroud image feature.

Responsive Design

Theme is mobile friendly. Adapts automatically when viewing on iPhones, iPads, and other small hand-held devices.

Optimized for Speed

We keep on updating theme codes with best latest theme development practices to keep the site loading time to minimum.

Footer Editor

With footer editor option you can change the footer copyright information with your custom footer info. Easily change it from theme customise section.

Sticky Menu

Supports sticky menu. This ensures that your visitors can navigate your site easily regardless of their position on the page.

Incredible Support

We provide free support to our free theme users via support forum. Pro theme users can send emails and support ticket as well.

Social Icons

Includes bunch of Social Icons options that can easily be integrated on the top header bar and footer. These icons will help you to connect with your social profiles.

Custom CSS

You can write custom css to tweak the look and the design of the theme as you like. It will remain there even when you update the theme.

Demo Content

With demo content you can make your site just like our demo site in few minutes and start editing your content from there. Here’s the demo content tutorial.

Translation Ready

The theme is fully translation ready. Meaning any hardcoded English text in our theme can be translated to your native language with ease.

WPML Compatible

The theme is fully compatible with WPML plugin in case you need to create a multilingual site.

Well Documented

We provide clear documentation for all our themes. These theme instruction will tell you how to use the theme features step by step.
