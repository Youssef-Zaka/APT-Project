Bike to Wherever Days 2022

As part of Bike to Work Days May 20th celebration, cyclists are encouraged to register for Bike to Wherever Days, which starts May 1 and runs through the entire month of May.

Express Lanes: Take the survey by May 20th

A low-income toll discount pilot program, lower toll violation penalties and more are being considered for the I-880 and I-680 Contra Costa express lanes. For more information, click here.

US-101 Express Lanes Are Tolling As Of February 11, 2022

As of Friday February 11th, Tolling on the US-101 Express Lanes in San Mateo and Santa Clara Counties has started. Learn what you need to know to use the new express lanes.

New Open Data Exchange Principles

MTC and a dozen other organizations have co-authored a set of principles for implementing open data exchange throughout the transit industry. For more information, click here.
