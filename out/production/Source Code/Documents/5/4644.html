The Annual Meeting of the Association of University Presses is the premier professional development and networking event for university press and nonprofit scholarly publishers. Held in June each year, the AUPresses meeting brings together hundreds of scholarly publishing professionals and partners to share ideas, combine knowledge, and make connections. AUPresses announces exciting and significant changes to the AUPresses Annual Meeting starting in 2022. More >

2022 Annual Meeting

AUPresses 2022 is scheduled to take place on June 18-20 in Washington, DC.
More 2022 >

2021 Annual Meeting

AUPresses 2021 was held virtually from June 7-18.
More 2021 >

2020 Annual Meeting

Due to the COVID-19 public health threat, #AUPresses20 became a virtual event held over 2 weeks in June.
More 2020 >

History of the Meeting

The annual meeting predates the Association! Our community of more than 150 scholarly publishers grew out of informal annual get-togethers of about 20 university presses in the early 1900s.
